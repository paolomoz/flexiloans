What is an E-Commerce Business Loan?

E-commerce businesses have unique requirements such as online compliance, lead generation, conversion, and delivery. To meet these requirements, an enterprise needs fast and easily accessible funding options. Hence, different types of lenders offer an online business loan called an e-commerce loan to meet an online business's specific requirements and promote business in the e-commerce sector. These loans are available at an attractive rate and can cover expenses like expansion, designing a website, brand promotion, etc.

Features of an E-Commerce Business Loan

An e-commerce loan is specially designed for online businesses. Some of its striking features are given below.

  • These are unsecured business loans and do not require any collateral.
  • An e-commerce business can avail of loans upto ₹50 Lakh.
  • These are short-term loans with a tenure of up to 270 days.
  • These loans have low-interest rates starting from 1.5%.
  • These loans offer flexible repayment schedule options.
  • The loan amount depends on the sales revenue per month

Eligibility to Avail of An E-Commerce Business Loan

An e-commerce business loan is available for anyone having an online business, a partnership firm, or a business with a presence on an e-commerce platform. To get an e-commerce business loan, businesses or business owners must fulfill certain eligibility criteria such as:

  • The business owner should be a citizen of India.
  • The online seller should have a registered business with a minimum of 6 months of operation.
  • The business should fulfill the minimum requirement of per month revenue as specified by the lender.
  • The business should have a strong presence on any popular e-commerce platform like Amazon, Flipkart, etc.
  • The business should have a good credit score for better chances of loan approval.

Selecting the Right E-Commerce Loan For Your Business

For your business's success, selecting the correct type of e-commerce loan that can fulfil your needs is crucial. Conduct market research and compare various loan options available in the market based on different parameters. Here are some key factors to consider while selecting an e-commerce loan:

  • Determine the right amount of loan you need for your business. Calculate all your monthly expenses and sales, and then identify your short-term and long-term goals. This will help you in estimating the correct loan value you need.
  • Compare interest rates and loan processing fees of various lenders. There is a lot of competition in this market. Hence you can leverage this opportunity to get a loan at the lowest interest rates and processing fees.
  • Look for lenders that provide fast loan disbursals with minimum paperwork.
  • Also, check the repayment schedule of various lenders. It is always good to choose a lender offering a flexible repayment schedule.
  • Check the terms and conditions of the loan. Different types of lenders have different terms and conditions. Some may charge interest, while some may prefer an equity share. Choose what suits you best.
