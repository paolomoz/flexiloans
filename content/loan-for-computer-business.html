Profitable startup ideas for computer enthusiasts

COMPUTER STORE

It is an exceedingly profitable enterprise in the industry. The ever-widening IT industry facilitates a strong foundation for computer stores or retail showrooms. Additionally, the computers and its peripherals have a stable demand as with each anticipated product launch customers keep on craving for newer products. Thus, the business is highly self-rewarding and requires you to put sizeable capital funding.

COMPUTER ASSEMBLING

This is the industry's most gainful enterprise. Investing in computer assembling is one of the smartest ideas for computer savvy person. The computer assembling business entails to providing customized PC making services. The components required for this business can be easily procured either from domestic or overseas markets. However, the business will require you to make a considerable investment.

COMPUTER ACCESSORIES STORE

Computer parts retailing is another moneymaking business in India. The venture deals with selling various parts and accessories of computer which may include a wide range of products such as motherboard, keyboard, mouse, charger, hard disk, processor, battery, etc. The business can be initialized with moderate capital. Furthermore, selling these add-ons can sometimes enable you to fetch higher earnings rather than the primary products.
