How to Apply for Kirana Store Loans

The application process for Kirana store loans is usually straightforward. Most lenders have an online application process that can be completed in a few minutes.

Borrowers will usually need to provide the following documents:

  1. PAN card
  2. Aadhar card
  3. Bank statements
  4. Income tax returns
  5. KYC documents

Once the application is complete, the lender will assess the borrower's eligibility and offer terms and conditions. If the borrower accepts the offer, the loan amount will be disbursed to the borrower's account within a few days.

Eligibility Criteria for Kirana Store Loan

The eligibility criteria for Kirana store loans vary from lender to lender, but usually involve the following:

  • The borrower must be an Indian citizen aged 21 years or above
  • The borrower must have a valid PAN card
  • The borrower must have a minimum annual turnover of Rs. 1 lakh
  • The borrower must have been in business for at least 1 year
  • The borrower must have a good credit score
