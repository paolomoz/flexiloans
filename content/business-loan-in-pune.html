What is a Business Loan?

Business loans are a popular form of financing that businesses can use to support their operations, growth, and projects. Banks or other financial institutions usually issue loans, charging interest rates and requiring businesses to make repayments through Equated Monthly Installments (EMI).

Businesses may avail of business loans for various reasons, including procuring fixed assets, expanding their working capital, consolidating debt, and hiring new staff. Small business owners looking for financial support can apply for small business loans to achieve their goals. Eligibility Criteria for Business Loans

The following are the eligibility criteria for business loans:

  1. Age: The minimum age for applying for a loan is 21 years, and the maximum age is 65 years.
  2. Income: The annual revenue of the business should be at least 10 lakhs or above. The business should have been profitable in the last year.
  3. Business Stability: We at FlexiLoans take several factors into account when approving a business loan. Business stability is one of the key factors that we consider. A business with an established track record is more likely to get the application approved. The minimum business vintage is 1 year, and the business experience must be 2 years.
  4. Outstanding Debts: Businesses that have high debt in their financial statements are more likely to get a lesser amount approved. The amount of loan sanctioned is determined based on the repayment capacity of the business, which again varies from business-to-business.
  5. Creditworthiness: An applicant with a higher CIBIL score is more likely to get the loan approved. A CIBIL score of 700 or more is generally considered good for obtaining a loan, but the minimum that we consider is 650. The amount of loan approved and the interest rates charged also depend on the credit score.
  6. Other Factors: The applicant should be an Indian citizen and should not have defaulted on any previous loan. The applicant should also own a home, office, shop, or godown.
