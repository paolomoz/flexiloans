Eligibility Criteria for MSME Loan

Businesses and individuals meeting the following criteria are eligible to apply:

  1. Business Type
    • Sole Proprietorships
    • Partnership Firms
    • Private and Public Limited Companies
    • Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs)
    • Self-employed professionals
    • Retailers, Traders, and Manufacturers
  2. Business Vintage
    • Your business must be operational for at least one year.
    • A stable business history improves loan approval chances.
  3. Annual Turnover
    • The minimum turnover requirement is ₹24 lakh per annum.
    • Businesses with higher revenue may qualify for larger loan amounts.
  4. Applicant Age
    • The business owner must be between 21 and 65 years old.
  5. Credit Score
    • A CIBIL score of 700 or above improves approval chances.
    • A strong financial record indicates good repayment capacity.
  6. Business Registration
    • Your business must be legally registered and have a valid GST certificate.
  7. Banking and Financial History
    • You must provide a minimum of six months' bank statements.
    • Consistent transactions and healthy balances improve approval odds.

Common Reasons MSME Loan Applications Get Rejected

1. Incomplete Documents

Unsigned or missing documents can lead to rejection.

2. Mismatched Business Information

GST, Udyam, and banking details must match.

3. Low or Unverified Turnover

Lenders need consistent revenue proof.

4. Poor Credit History

A low CIBIL score can reduce approval chances.

5. Expired Registrations

Outdated licenses or registrations impact credibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the minimum turnover required for an MSME loan?

Most lenders look for monthly turnover of ₹2–5 lakhs or annual turnover starting from ₹25 lakhs.

2. Can I apply for an MSME loan without collateral?

Yes, many digital lenders offer collateral-free MSME loans.

3. Is GST registration mandatory?

Not always, but it improves eligibility and approval chances.

4. Can startups apply for MSME loans?

Yes, if they meet minimum business vintage and turnover requirements.
