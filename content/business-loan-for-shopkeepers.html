Shop Loan Fees & Interest Rates

The following table indicates the approximated charges and interest rates levied by the lender:

PROCESSING FEE
This charge is taken by the lender to complete the loan. It generally represents a percentage of the loan balance and might be between 1% and 5%.
INTEREST RATE BY THE LENDER
The lender's interest rate will be determined by several criteria, including the borrower's credit score, the kind of loan, and the loan conditions. Rates might range between 5% and 30%.
CLOSING CHARGES
These are the closing costs, which include title insurance, attorney's fees, and recording expenses. They might range anywhere between 2% and 5% of the loan price.
MONTHLY MAINTENANCE FEE
The lender charges this monthly fee to keep the loan account active. It might range between 0.025% and 0.075% every month.
