Important Things to Keep in Mind Before Applying for a Business Loan for Your Shop

For those, who don’t know how to get a business loan for their shop, here are the important prerequisites for business loan eligibility. 

  • It is important to have a good credit score
  • In case you already have existing small business loans, make sure that you have not defaulted on any of your business loan EMIs before
  • Ensure that you have all the required documents ready when applying for the business loan for your shop
  • The interested individual who wants to take up a loan for his shop must be at least 21 years old age
  • The age of the applicant must not exceed, in any case, 65 years
  • The individual needs to be a resident of India for availing of the loan amount 
  • Also, the applicant needs to have certain important documents for the successful transfer of the loan. These are your PAN card, proof of address, prior 6 months of bank statements, your income proof along with Business proof
  • The lender may even ask for your credit score. Thus, it is important on the borrower’s part to have a credit score of at least 650 and above

Advantages of Taking a Business Loan for Shopkeepers 

Here is a list of advantages of taking a business loan for shopkeepers.

1. Lower Interest Rates

The best part about availing of a loan as a shopkeeper is the lower interest rate on loans. Although, interest rates can vary depending on the lender and your eligibility as a retail store or a shop owner. To calculate the exact loan interest rate, you can use a business loan EMI calculator that will help you know the exact rate of interest. With this, you will be able to figure out the exact amount that you need for your business expansion. 

2. Flexible Repayment Terms

Availing of a loan comes with several perks if you are a shopkeeper. A flexible repayment option is one of them. You can request the lender to offer a repayment schedule best suits your needs. With this, you will be able to pay back the amount at your own pace without any kind of stress in mind while focusing on your business growth. 

3. Tax Benefits

For the unversed, taking a loan for running your business helps you in saving taxes. And in case, you are running a small business like a retail shop then it may help you even save a good amount of money that may be required for an upfront licensing fee too. 

4. Online Application

Owing to the advancement in technology and product innovation, it is very easy for shopkeepers to apply for a business loan. You can even do it online. All you need to do is go to the respective website, fill in the required details, check your eligibility and then submit the form and ta-da, it’s done. Plus, the best part is that the documentation work required for a business loan for shopkeepers is hassle-free and also minimal.

5. Minimal Processing Fee

The processing fee for a business loan for shopkeepers is almost negligible. The prepayment penalty, in this case, is also minimal. With this, a shopkeeper can easily do a lot of healthy cost-cutting while being able to invest the amount on other significant things like business assets or hiring resources for upscaling the business. 

6. Collateral Not Required 

Business Loans do not require security or collateral such as a house mortgage. This gives the borrowers freedom to access other resources with the available capital and deploy it for the future benefit of the business. 

7. Working Capital 

It is the total money a business requires to carry out day-to-day operations smoothly, such as paying salaries and buying resources. A business loan can help you manage this working capital for stabilizing the enterprise when the flow of income takes a setback.  

8. Improved Credibility 

Taking a business loan from a shopkeeper not just helps them streamline their operations but also adds to the credibility of the business. This is owing to the feasible repayment options and low-interest rates. Needless to say, it is one of the key advantages of availing a business loan to shopkeepers. 

Wrapping Up 

No doubt that taking a loan is helpful for businesses at multiple levels. From small businesses like a shop or retail stores to medium and large enterprises like full-fledged companies, a business loan can help a business in getting a head start in the economy while minimizing its risk while upscaling its value. For the long-term success of a business, a business loan can really play its part.
