Challenges Faced by MSMEs in Tier 2 and Tier 3 Cities

Whether you need money to complete seasonal demand or pay for expenses, there is always a need for funds to run a business. MSME loans prove to be a saviour here. However, only 57% of the MSME loan applications are approved in Tier 2 and 3 cities in India.

MSMEs in smaller cities face many challenges that stop them from growing and making profits. Here are some MSME funding challenges in Tier 2 and 3 cities many businesses face:

  • Limited Access to Financing: This is one of the main challenges MSMEs face in small cities. The traditional banks mostly favour bigger businesses or those in metro cities and tend to leave the smaller city MSME underserved.
  • Low Financial Literacy: Most business owners do not know about the loan options available to them. Another challenge is that traditional banks have a long and complex process.
  • Complex Application Processes: Traditional banks often ask you to submit a lot of documents and paperwork. The process of applying for the loan itself can be difficult for small businesses, adding more to the financial difficulties for MSMEs.
  • Operational Scaling Challenges: If there is a lack of working capital, MSMEs cannot invest in their infrastructure, inventory, or new technology.

These problems are even higher in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities, where getting financial support is low as resources are very constrained. FlexiLoans for MSMEs brings innovative, user-friendly loans for Tier 2 MSMEs that cater to the small business segment and address their pain points.
