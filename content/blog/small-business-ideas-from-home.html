Top 10 Small Business Ideas From Home

1. Tailoring

Stitching and embroidery are things that never go out of style. We always like to dress and present ourselves with the latest trends in the market.

The Indian tailoring market is worth $27,000 crore and is expanding at a 12% annual rate.

In traditional tailoring, you meet the customers in a shop. But post-COVID, online tailoring is gaining momentum. Online tailoring can help your customers contact you from anywhere. They can also select how they want the dress to fit their body type online. You need a fashion niche, technology information, and a creative team to start this. A small investment is enough to start it at home. You can also apply for a business loan for the initial capital.

2. Digital marketing

The fastest-growing industry is digital marketing. Digital marketing services are in high demand post-pandemic. It is expanding from 25% to 30% per year. They are essential in increasing a company’s branding and marketing services. All you need to know are the main aspects of digital marketing. Some of them are SEO, content marketing, blogging, pay-per-click, and social media management. You can start a small business from home with a small business loan. So, if you enjoy planning and executing marketing strategies, this is your business.

3. Baking

Do you enjoy baking delicious goodies like pastries, brownies, and other confections? Then, a home-based bakery business is the best option for you. You can work from home and be your boss with a home-based business setup. Have a tempting menu and spend the initial investment on bakery equipment and ingredients. You are all set to start now. Maximize your marketing with the help of Instagram to advertise your business. On average, a small-scale baking business could earn between INR 60k and INR 1.2 lakhs per month. 4. Day-care centre or preschool

Leaving your children in a daycare center or preschool has become common practice in India. Because of their jobs, the majority of them live in nuclear families. So the child’s safety is important to them. Parents choose these facilities because they provide their children with various skills. At the same time, it creates a safer environment. Preschools help to lay the foundation for the child to build on. A good preschool learning environment is important to increase a child’s curiosity and creativity. As per the facts, India’s childcare market will expand at a CAGR of about 12%. (2021-2026). Get a well-written, comprehensive business strategy and a valid registration and license to start your daycare center. 5. Photography 

Photography is now becoming a career that is taking a quantum leap. There are various types of photography, like candid photography, wedding photography, event photography, lifestyle photography, wildlife photography, food photography, fashion photography, and so on. Photography is one of those past times that has evolved into a career. The better the camera and optics, the more enriched the images will be. Everyone employs a professional photographer for weddings, birthdays, events, festivals, etc. This profession nowadays has a broad reach, and the commercial opportunity is promising. It’s essential to have first-hand experience and a great photography portfolio. Have trust in your accuracy and photographic skill to become a good photographer and win accolades and money. If you want to freelance, you can earn up to INR 375 per hour. Senior photographers who freelance can earn up to INR 1,000,000 per month with experience. 6. Home cooking services

You can turn home-cooked food service into a business if you master the art of cooking. You can count on your talent, as all you need are the resources to prepare and deliver it. Home-cooked food services are still a relatively new type of business in urban and semi-urban cities. There is a high need for them. People’s schedules are usually very hectic in busy cities. All they want is peace of mind while eating delicious food. Many people do not even have time to prepare food for themselves. With home-cooked services, you can deliver to many office workers who cannot find the time to prepare a lunch at home. You can extend those services as packages to older couples who cannot cook daily. You can earn a monthly salary of Rs. 70,000 to Rs. 80,000 after covering your costs. You can get a working capital loan to start your service from home on a small scale. 

7. Personalized gift shop 

If you have creative talent and can customize things, start your gift shop from home. Personalized gifts are always thoughtful and add a personal touch. When someone sends a personalized gift, it enhances the relationship. Gifts manifest true emotions and convey appreciation to the people who receive them. With your talent and creativity, you can help enhance your customers’ relationships by curating gifts as they like. Having a website where you can display these curated gifts to attract customers helps your business. Most common personalized gifts include a diverse range of coffee mugs, cushions, photo cakes, photo frames, key holders, pen stands, small stands, candle holders, and so on. Start your social media channels to market your gifts and attract new sales and customers. You can earn from Rs. 300 to Rs. 4000 per gift based on your talent, skill, and knowledge of personalizing things. You can take out a loan by applying for an online business loan. Be unique and enjoy the perks of being artistic. 

8. Fitness Specialist

If you are passionate about fitness, starting a personal fitness training business is low-cost and highly profitable. Everyone has become a fitness enthusiast, irrespective of age and work. We all want to be fit and fine.

You can start from home with fitness training like yoga, Zumba, aerobics, and pilates, along with tips on nutritional food habits. You can do workshops and classes offline and online, with schedules to attract many enthusiasts. With skill and expertise, a trainer can earn a minimum of around INR 50000 per month. Aside from that, you can start a YouTube channel to share your fitness videos and earn money from them.

9. Web designer

Post-pandemic, all jobs have become mostly online for better customer reach. The once-offline store has gone online to increase sales. So the majority of companies in today’s world need to have websites with domain names. If you are tech-savvy and have web development and design knowledge, you can start your web design business at home. Every company’s desire to develop a website for the goods or services they are dealing with has increased the demand for web designers. You only need a computer and experience, so starting a business doesn’t involve a significant investment. Once you start your business with website design, you can approach nearby start-ups or established companies to see if they want to create a new website from scratch or change one that already exists. You can earn estimated annual earnings of INR 4,00,000 to INR 6,00,000 based on your skill level, expertise, and knowledge.

10. Organic terrace farming

Everyone is aware of the pesticides used on vegetables and fruits in today’s world. They want to consume fresh food without any chemicals. So, the popularity of organic and terrace farming is growing among the public. People are searching for good organic staples online. You can create a terrace garden on your rooftop or balcony with flowers, spices, healthy fruits, and vegetables. The investments include proper terrace planning, pots, compost soil, and requirements related to terrace farming, like a trowel, shovel, watering can or hosepipe, pruning scissors, and a gardening rake. It’s not only a better business option but also good for stress. You can also upload your videos on YouTube channels like “tips and tricks of terrace farming” and monetize from them there too. Income from terrace farming ranges from INR 80,000 to INR 90,000 per month. You can apply for term loans or business loans for the initial investment.
