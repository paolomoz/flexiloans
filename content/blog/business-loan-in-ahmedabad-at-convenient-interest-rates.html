Who is Eligible to Apply for a Business Loan in Ahmedabad?

A business loan can be availed by anyone operating a profitable business or who aspires to start one. Different banks use different criteria to determine loan eligibility and the amount sanctioned. Some of those criteria are

  • Type of business — sole proprietorship, partnership, a private company, or a public limited company.
  • The company’s sales
  • The company’s income
  • The business’s financial flow
  • The company’s past record and activities

General Eligibility Criteria and Conditions for Salaried and Self-Employed Loan Applicants

For Salaried Applicants:

  • The applicant’s workplace should be specified.
  • An applicant must earn at least INR 25,000 per month.
  • The applicant must be aged between 21 and 65 years.
  • Three years of work experience and earnings must be deposited only in their bank accounts.
  • Good credit history or CIBIL score.

Self-Employed Applicants

  • The individual’s credit scores should be good, and they should have no outstanding financial liabilities.
  • The annual income should be at least Rs 2.5 lakh.
  • The applicants must produce their financial and bank statements for the previous three months as part of the business loan documentation process.
  • The minimum age requirement for business loan applicants is 25 years old.
  • Applicants seeking a business loan should have an ITR file for the previous three years.
  • The company should have been operating continuously for the previous three years.

Requirement of CIBIL Score for Business Loan Ahmedabad

The minimum CIBIL score necessary for processing a business loan application in Ahmedabad varies. A CIBIL score of 750 is required to process the business loan application for most companies. Aside from the CIBIL score, companies look at previous loan behaviour in detail. This includes the type of loan, amount received and repayment history.
