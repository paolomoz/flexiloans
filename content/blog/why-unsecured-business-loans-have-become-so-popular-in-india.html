Why Have Unsecured Business Loans Become So Popular in India?

Posted May 30, 2022 · Updated Jan 14, 2026 · 5 min read

Unsecured Business Loans are loans that are offered without any collateral. These loans do not require security. They are short-term, small loans provided to cover periodic expenditures, raw material costs, and vendor payments, i.e., these loans are used for working capital management. Furthermore, these loans are also be used to expand a business. However, since unsecured business loans mainly cover immediate expenses, it is better to use them for short-term goals. This way, it will increase the business return profits. 

Unsecured Business loans come into the picture mainly in cases of small businesses, especially the businesses in need of urgent profits. Furthermore, business owners who cannot sell their assets to get a loan also find Unsecured Business Loans beneficial. 

Reasons Why Unsecured Business Loans Have Become So Popular in India

In recent years, there has been a rise in the popularity of Unsecured Business Loans in India. Listed below are some reasons for the rising popularity of Unsecured Business Loans in India:

  1. Resolves temporary business disruptions: The business market in current times is highly unpredictable and volatile. Unsecured Business Loans are an apt way of boosting small business owners’ confidence by providing them with stable working capital loans. Therefore, unsecured business loans stabilise the market and give business owners access to working capital to solve temporary business disruptions due to the changing and unpredictable business market. 
  2. Positive Working Capital:  It is essential to have positive working capital to have a stable business. But it is often seen that business owners have trouble conducting a smooth running of their businesses. There can be various problems with the raw materials, daily costs, salary payments, rent payments etc. Unsecured Business Loans help diminish these problems and help gain a positive working capital and, subsequently, the smooth running of businesses. 
  3. Faster Loan Approvals: One of the main reasons for the rising popularity of Unsecured Business Loans is faster loan approvals. Due to the uncertainty of the business market, sometimes business owners, especially small business owners or business owners facing massive losses, require business loans on an urgent basis. Unsecured Business Loans are custom made to cater to the needs of such business owners. Hence, they are easier to access and therefore more preferred by business owners. 
  4. Using funds flexibly: A traditional bank puts many restrictions while giving out business loans. Some lenders even require detailed plans before they give the loan. On the contrary, an Unsecured Business Loan does not put any limits on the use of the loan. It allows business owners to use the loan for their purpose without hesitation. Most lenders who provide the business loans do not ask why they are taking the loan. They do not require any specification about how the business owners will use the loans. Therefore, this has made Unsecured Business Loans popular in India. 
  5. Business Growth: Stable growth is essential in every business. Business owners often take business loans to increase their business. But business loan requirements are pretty complicated and time-consuming. However, Unsecured Business Loan Requirements are not complex or rigid and are easy to access. Therefore, business owners can easily use these loans to expand their businesses. However, since Unsecured Business Loans are customised short time loans, it is essential to use them for immediate investments for profits. 
  6. Easy Repayment Options: It is necessary to maintain cash flow and profits for businesses to function. Without a stable cash flow, companies will have to maintain a proper payment schedule. A traditional business loan requires an appropriate payment schedule in which the business owners will have to pay the necessary amount to clear the loan. However, without proper business earnings, the business will not be able to repay the loan on time. However, with Unsecured Business Loans, the payment schedule is quite flexible. Subsequently, the business owners will be able to pay monthly instalments of their loan according to their business earnings. 
  7. Earn Credit Ratings:  Earning credit points is also an important reason for the rise of Unsecured Business Loans. Generally, moneylenders and banks check credit ratings before loaning a certain amount. Bad credit ratings sometimes become an obstacle while applying for a business loan. However, Unsecured Business Loan requirements allow business owners to take loans even when their credit ratings are bad. They can apply for an Unsecured Business Loan and eventually improve the credit ratings of the business. This way, small businesses or businesses suffering from loss will be eligible to apply for a loan. 
  8. Flexible Terms and Conditions: Business Loans generally have strict terms and conditions, from application and repayment requirements. Therefore, it becomes problematic for small business owners to take a business loan to complete their payments or expand their business. However, Unsecured Business Loans have flexible requirements and terms and conditions like pre-payment charges, low processing fees, auto-renewal of a loan after repayment etc. Therefore, it is much more convenient to take an unsecured business loan to enhance, improve and ensure the smooth running of businesses. Flexible terms and conditions are also one of the main reasons for the rising popularity of Unsecured Business Loans in India. 

Conclusion

Unsecured Business Loans are a boon to small business owners and business owners struggling financially. These business owners can take a loan and spend it bettering their businesses. Since it is pretty flexible and easy to access, it has become the obvious choice of business owners in the past few years. In addition, the Unsecured Business Loan Requirements are not as rigid as a Business Loan, making the application process much easier and smoother. Furthermore, Unsecured Business Loans also help run businesses smoothly. Therefore, these are some of the main reasons for the rising popularity of Unsecured Business Loans.
