Benefits of applying for a business loan online

1. Paperless Online Application

There is no paperwork to do, and there is no need to fill out lengthy application forms. The application form is available online, and only requires the completion of a few fields. Soft paper copies that are only required for the application must be uploaded electronically along with the form. It allows the borrower to fill out the loan paperwork at any time using a mobile or computer.

2. Repayment Tenure

The repayment options on an online small business loan are flexible, making repayments easy. An instant business loan may be taken out for a period ranging from one year to five years. However, this will differ from one lender to the next.

3. Top-up Loan Program

If the borrowed funds are insufficient to meet your needs, you can obtain additional funds on the same debt through the top-up loan feature for current borrowers.

4. Criteria for Nominal Eligibility

Lenders that offer online loans have certain simple and limited eligibility requirements. In particular, when dealing with banks, the eligibility criteria set by them are challenging for small and medium-sized businesses to follow. They have a method for determining qualifications, which decreases their risk. On the other hand, online loans have the versatility that is not dependent on the CIBIL score or the borrower’s profile.

5. Interest Rates

Business loans are also cost-effective because they come with low interest rates. Once you equate the interest rates charged by online lenders to those charged by conventional lenders, you’ll see that they’re far lower.

6. Collateral Free

The majority of online business loans do not need collateral. This means unlike traditional bank business loans, you won’t have to put up any equity, or provide a guarantor to secure the loan.

Various Online Business Finance Alternatives

Term loans, lines of credit, and invoice funding are only some of the borrowing opportunities available by online lenders. Each one of these funding options has its own set of benefits.

1. Term Loans

In return for funding, term business loans include routine, fixed payments, and interest is paid. Term loans are generally referred to as “traditional” loans.

Benefits:

  • The application process is streamlined.
  • Interest rates are frequently affordable.
  • No equity and sales must be surrendered.

2. Credit Line

A credit line is similar to a credit card. Your company will be given a credit limit, and you will be able to withdraw funds if required before the line of credit is met.

Benefits:

  • Allows you to get cash quickly and easily (once approved).
  • It gives you financial freedom.
  • Pay terms are flexible. You need to pay interest just on the money you’ve drawn out.

Invoice Funding

Accounts receivable lending is similar to a cash advance, except that you will be selling an item rather than getting a loan. You’ll offer invoices in return for funding in this situation.

Benefits:

  • There is no need for collateral.
  • The lender assumes the burden of receiving receivables.
  • There is no need for up-front collateral.
