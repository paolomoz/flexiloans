If you’re looking to apply for an Aadhaar PVC card or check your PVC Aadhaar card status, this comprehensive guide will walk you through the process.

What is an Aadhaar PVC Card?

The Aadhaar PVC card is a compact, wallet-friendly version of the Aadhaar card, made from polyvinyl chloride (PVC) and resembling the size of a credit or debit card. It is designed with enhanced security features, making it more durable and resistant to tampering than the traditional paper Aadhaar card. This makes the PVC Aadhaar card a more convenient and sturdy alternative, while maintaining the same validity as other Aadhaar formats, including the paper-based card, e-Aadhaar, m-Aadhaar and masked e-Aadhaar.

Aadhaar PVC Card Security Features

  1. The Aadhaar PVC card comes with the official Aadhaar logo embossed on the surface.
  2. The PVC Aadhaar card displays a ghost image (a faint reproduction) of your photograph visible against light.
  3. Micro text printed on the Aadhaar PVC card is readable only with magnification.
  4. The PVC Aadhaar card features a complex hologram. It makes the card tamper-proof.
  5. The PVC Aadhaar card mentions the date of printing and issuance.
  6. A secure QR code on the Aadhaar PVC card stores all your demographic details.
  7. The intricate Guilloche pattern on the PVC Aadhaar card is difficult to forge.

Benefits of the Aadhaar PVC Card

  1. Durability: Made of high-quality PVC material, the Aadhaar PVC card is more resistant to wear and tear. It ensures a longer lifespan compared to the regular paper Aadhaar card.
  2. Enhanced Security: The PVC Aadhaar card features advanced security elements like a hologram, micro text, ghost image, secure QR code, etc., making it difficult to forge or duplicate.
  3. Ease of Use: The compact, credit card-sized PVC Aadhaar card is more convenient to carry in your wallet or purse.
  4. Offline Verification: The Aadhaar PVC card’s security features enable easy offline verification, eliminating the need for an internet connection during authentication.

Difference Between Aadhaar PVC Card and Paper Aadhaar Card

  • Material: The PVC Aadhaar card is made of durable polyvinyl chloride, while the paper Aadhaar card is made of laminated paper.
  • Size: The Aadhaar PVC card is compact and easy to carry, similar to a credit card, whereas the paper Aadhaar card is larger.
  • Security Features: The PVC Aadhaar card boasts advanced security features like a hologram, ghost image and secure QR code, making it more tamper-proof and difficult to forge compared to the paper Aadhaar card.

Eligibility and Requirements to Apply for an Aadhaar PVC Card

To apply for an Aadhaar PVC card, you must have a valid 12-digit Aadhaar number. You can use your registered mobile number to place an order for the PVC Aadhaar card. You can still apply for the PVC Aadhaar card using a non-registered or alternate mobile number if you have not registered your mobile number with UIDAI.

Documents Required to Apply for an Aadhaar PVC Card

Since you are applying for a PVC Aadhaar card against your existing Aadhaar number, no additional documents are needed. You just need to have your Aadhaar number and mobile number handy to apply for an Aadhaar PVC card.

How to Apply for an Aadhaar PVC Card Online

  1. Visit the official UIDAI website and click on “Order Aadhaar PVC Card” under the “Get Aadhaar” section.
  2. If you have registered your mobile number with UIDAI, log in to your myAadhaar account using your 12-digit Aadhaar number, and the system will send an OTP to your registered mobile number. You can still proceed with the PVC Aadhaar card application using an alternate mobile number if you have not registered your mobile number.
  3. Verify your demographic details (name, date of birth, gender and address) displayed on the screen and click “Submit.”
  4. Confirm your request and make the payment of Rs ₹50 (inclusive of GST and Speed Post charges) using any available online payment method.
  5. After successful payment, you will receive an acknowledgement containing your Service Request Number (SRN). Download and save this acknowledgement for future reference and to check your PVC Aadhaar card status.

How to Check Your Aadhaar PVC Card Status

  1. Visit the official UIDAI website and click on “Check Aadhaar PVC Card Status” under the “Get Aadhaar” section.
  2. Enter your 28-digit Service Request Number (SRN) and the captcha code, then click “Submit” to view your PVC card status.

Aadhaar PVC Card Delivery Time and Process

Once you successfully apply for the PVC Aadhaar card, UIDAI will process your request and hand over the PVC Aadhaar card to India Post within 5 working days (excluding the date of request).

Speed Post will deliver the PVC Aadhaar card to your Aadhaar-registered address, usually within 5 to 15 working days.

What to Do After Receiving Your PVC Aadhaar Card

  1. Check that all the information mentioned on the card is correct, including your name, date of birth, gender, address and photograph.
  2. Safely store your PVC Aadhaar card in a wallet or cardholder to prevent damage or loss.
  3. Use your PVC Aadhaar card as a valid proof of identity. The Aadhaar card is one of the most important documents required for business loans, MSME loans, opening a bank account or wherever you need to produce an address proof.
