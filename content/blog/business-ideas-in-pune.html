List of top Small Business Ideas in Pune

Technical support services

Being an IT hub, the IT companies in Pune require instant and constant hardware and software support. Setting up a technical support and assistance services business is very lucrative since you can quickly get clients after setting up your business. In the beginning, you can start with a small investment, and as you grow your business and get a solid client base, you can use SME business loans to expand your business across the city.

Bike and car rental services

With its good weather and proximity to scenic locations such as Lonavala and Khadakwasla, Pune attracts a lot of tourists. Renting a bike and car is usually something that helps tourists to visit such places at their convenience. This is a great area to set up a business. With the help of a collateral-free business loan or unsecured business loan, you can easily set up such a business in Pune.

Real estate or architectural firm

Pune, in recent years, has become the top real estate investment city. Due to its calm and soothing weather all year round, it has caught the eye of many real estate developers and homebuyers. The real estate business offers big profit margins which can cover your business loans or MSME loans in the long run. You can even consider opening an architectural firm with tie-ups with real estate developers in auditing and designing the final project plan. Such business ideas in Pune with low investment can yield higher returns in the long run.

Real estate broker

Suppose you do not want to be a real estate developer or have no expertise in architectural nuances. In that case, you can still set up a real estate business by becoming a real estate broker. Being a real estate broker is great in Pune since it has many renowned colleges and corporate sectors that attract talent from India. That being said, accommodation is necessary for every individual who comes to Pune for work or study. So, as a real estate broker, you can get a lot of clients if you set shop near a residential area in proximity to a college or a corporate industrial area.

Cloud kitchen

This is a growing concept in Pune with the flourishing home delivery system. This new business idea in Pune of starting a food joint with only deliveries requires a lower investment than a full-fledged restaurant. Partnering with food delivery services like Swiggy and Zomato will give your cloud kitchen the required boost needed to generate revenue and cover your online business loans.

Electronics repairing shop

Electronics such as laptops and mobile phones are now necessary for every household. So, it is only natural that setting up a small business idea in Pune of electronic repairing will be profitable. Setting up an electronics repairing shop offering maintenance and servicing of old electrical appliances along with repairing any damage to any electronic equipment is one of the best business ideas in Pune with low investment.

Playschool

Opening up a playschool can be a worthwhile investment in Pune since many families have working parents. Hence, reliable daycare is a necessity for parents to provide for their child’s overall nurturing and sound moral and linguistic education early on.

Medical stores or path labs

Running a medical store is an evergreen business with guaranteed revenue. You can embellish it by providing value-added services such as doctor’s chambers and testing facilities. Furthermore, you can offer doorstep delivery of medicines and sample collection to cater to the needs of every customer.
