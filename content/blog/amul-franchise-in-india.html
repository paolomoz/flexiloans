What are the selling points of an Amul Franchise? 

  1. Value for customers – Amul’s brand ensures a credible value for its customers through its support and other significant benefits at low costs.
  2. Broad Target Audience – Amul has a vast target audience and caters to restaurants, cafes, fast food shops, sweet shops and families. Some of these businesses have also expanded to online stores.
  3. Limited Market Competition – The primary product that Amul deals with is milk and dairy products. This sector has competitors like Kwality Walls, Vadilal, and Mother Dairy. However, Amul holds the largest market share in numerous product categories. 

What Are the Requirements to Set Up an Amul Franchise?

Here’s how to get amul franchise:

1. Requirements of Land – There are different space requirements for an Amul franchise in India. For instance – for a standard Amul parlour outlet, 100 to 150 square feet of area is needed. An ice cream parlour needs 250 to 400 square feet of space. An area of just 50 to 100 square feet is required to set up an outlet at the railway station. 

2. Types of equipment – After fulfilling the land requirements, it is important to ensure that all the required equipment is on site. This includes deep freezers, refrigerators, pizza ovens, cone handlers, and milk coolers.

How much does an Amul Parlour Franchise Cost in India?

The cost of Amul parlour in India varies depending on the type of outlet. 

  • To open an Amul milk outlet, a capital cost of at least ₹25,000 as security is needed. In addition, the land and equipment costs can amount to up to ₹80,000 each.
  • To open an Amul ice cream parlour, a capital cost of ₹50,000 as security is needed. In addition, the land cost of ₹3,00,000 and the equipment cost of ₹1,50,000 will also need to be factored in.
  • To set up a railway Amul parlour, a potential franchise owner will need ₹1,00,000 as security, ₹2,50,000- ₹4,00,000 for land, and ₹50,000 for equipment. 

It must be noted that in return for these total costs, Amul’s franchise profit ranges between ₹5,00,000 to ₹10,00,000.

How to Apply for the Amul Franchise?

Here are the steps to apply for the Amul franchise in India:

  1. Visit the Amul website.
  2. At the bottom right, find and click on the Amul Parlours option.
  3. Select the ‘Online Form for Amul Parlour’ option from the list.
  4. Fill in the required details. Carefully check the details after entering and click on ‘Submit’.
  5. After successfully submitting the application form, you will receive a confirmation email at the registered email id. This email will also communicate further steps to set up the business. 

The Amul customer care number helps solve any queries. 

It must be noted that there are fake and unauthorised Amul websites. Therefore, ensure that the official Amul website is used for all the application processes.
