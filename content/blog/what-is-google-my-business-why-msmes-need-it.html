How to Set Up a Google My Business (GMB) Profile?

Setting up a Google business listing for MSMEs is simple. Here’s how MSMEs can create and optimise their profile:

Step 1: Sign Up on Google My Business

  • Visit the Google My Business for MSMEs website.
  • Click “Manage Now” and log in using a Google account.

Step 2: Enter Business Information

  • Provide accurate details, including business name, address, phone number, category, and website.
  • Ensure consistency across all online platforms for improved GMB profile optimisation.

Step 3: Verify Your Business

  • Google verifies the listing via postcard, phone, or email.
  • Enter the verification code to activate the profile.
  • This step is crucial in improving Google My Business ranking tips.

Step 4: Optimise Your Profile

  • Upload high-quality images and videos.
  • Add relevant keywords for local SEO with Google My Business.
  • Enable messaging and appointment booking to enhance customer experience.

Step 5: Regularly Update and Monitor Your GMB Profile

  • Respond to reviews and customer queries.
  • Post updates, promotions, and business announcements.
  • Monitor insights to refine marketing strategies.

Features of Google My Business for MSMEs

For MSMEs, Google My Business for small businesses serves as a powerful digital tool that enhances online visibility, strengthens customer relationships, and provides valuable insights into consumer behaviour.

1. Free Online Visibility and Local SEO Benefits

A key Google My Business benefit is free online visibility, helping MSMEs appear in search results without costly advertising. This aligns with effective marketing strategies for small businesses in 2025. A well-optimised Google business listing for MSMEs boosts local search rankings, attracting more customers, especially for businesses relying on foot traffic, like restaurants and retail stores.

For example, a grocery store with a complete Google My Business for MSMEs profile, including keywords, accurate location, and reviews, is more likely to appear in “near me” searches. Even without a website, MSMEs can use Google My Business for small businesses as a mini-site to share essential details and engage with customers directly.

2. Enhanced Customer Engagement

Effective customer engagement is key to business success, and Google My Business benefits MSMEs by offering multiple ways to interact with customers. Features such as messaging, Q&A, and review responses enable businesses to address queries, resolve complaints, and build relationships.

  • Messaging Feature: MSMEs can enable direct messaging, allowing potential customers to inquire about product availability, services, or pricing in real-time.
  • Google Q&A: Customers can ask questions directly on the business profile, and the business owner (or even other users) can provide answers.
  • Review Management: Responding to reviews demonstrates to customers that the business values their feedback, which in turn improves trust and credibility.

For instance, a café responding to a negative review by addressing the issue and inviting the customer back for a better experience demonstrates excellent customer service. This can turn dissatisfied customers into loyal patrons.

3. Google Maps Integration for Better Discoverability

A business without an online presence may struggle to attract customers. However, Google My Business for MSMEs allows businesses to be easily found on Google Maps, providing clear directions to their location.

When a customer searches for services nearby, Google displays a Google business listing for MSMEs in a local pack. This list includes essential details, such as contact number, operating hours, and a map pin, making it easy for customers to navigate to the business.

For example, if someone searches “car repair shop near me,” businesses with a correctly set up Google My Business for small businesses profile will appear on the map with directions, customer ratings, and photos.

This feature is especially beneficial for businesses with physical locations such as hotels, gyms, clinics, and showrooms. Those that update their listing frequently with accurate location details experience higher foot traffic and inquiries.

4. Insights and Analytics for Strategic Decision-Making

One of the most valuable Google My Business benefits is access to detailed analytics. MSMEs can track customer interactions, profile views, and engagement metrics, enabling them to refine their marketing strategies.

The Google My Business for MSMEs insights dashboard provides data on:

  • How customers discover the business (direct search, discovery search, or branded search).
  • The number of calls, website visits, and direction requests generated by the listing.
  • Popular search terms used by customers to find the business.

For instance, if a retail store notices a surge in searches for “organic skincare products,” it can adjust its product offerings to meet customer demand. These insights enable data-driven decision-making, allowing MSMEs to tailor their strategies for better results.

5. Google Posts for Promotions and Announcements

The Google My Business for MSMEs platform features a “Posts” section that enables businesses to share updates, promotions, and events directly on their profile. These posts appear in search results, making them an effective way to engage customers.

Businesses can post about:

  • Special offers or discounts
  • Upcoming events or workshops
  • New product launches
  • Business updates, such as changes in operating hours

For example, a clothing boutique can post about an upcoming sale with a call-to-action, such as “Visit us this weekend for 30% off all items!” This increases engagement and drives more foot traffic.

6. Photos and Videos to Showcase Products and Services

Visual content plays a crucial role in attracting customers. Google My Business benefits MSMEs by enabling them to upload high-quality images and videos, making their offerings more appealing. This not only enhances their online presence but also helps build trust and engagement, ultimately driving more inquiries and sales. Businesses that regularly update their photos see higher engagement.

For instance, a restaurant with well-lit, appealing images of its dishes will attract more diners than one without any images. MSMEs should upload:

  • Exterior and interior photos of their business location
  • Product images showcasing different offerings
  • Team photos to create a personal connection
  • Videos demonstrating services or behind-the-scenes operations

By leveraging the power of visual content, MSMEs can enhance their Google business listing for MSMEs and make a lasting impression on potential customers.

7. Appointment Booking for Service-Based Businesses

For service-based businesses, such as salons, clinics, and consultants, this offers a valuable appointment booking feature. This not only allows customers to schedule appointments directly from the listing but also streamlines the booking process, reducing friction and significantly improving conversions. As a result, businesses can enhance customer convenience while increasing their overall efficiency and engagement.

For example, a dental clinic can integrate an appointment booking link, making it easy for patients to book a slot without needing to visit a separate website. This convenience improves customer experience and increases bookings.

8. Local SEO Advantages and Higher Search Rankings

A well-optimised Google My Business for MSMEs listing contributes significantly to Google My Business ranking tips. Businesses that complete their profile, add relevant keywords, and actively engage with customers rank higher in search results.

  • Profile completeness (business name, address, phone number, and category).
  • Number and quality of customer reviews.
  • Frequency of updates (photos, posts, and responses).
  • Relevance to local search queries.

For example, a pet grooming salon that consistently updates its listing, responds to reviews, and shares posts about pet care tips is more likely to rank higher than a competitor with an inactive profile.
