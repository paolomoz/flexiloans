Government Schemes for Female Entrepreneurs in India

The government of India truly values the strength of businesswomen. To back their growth, funding for female entrepreneurs in India has increased over the years. These schemes meet the specific requirements of such businesses. Let us take a look at the various options that are available and how they can help:

  1. Annapurna Scheme
    If you dream of starting a food business, this is one of the best government schemes for female entrepreneurs in India that you must consider. You can borrow up to ₹50,000 to buy the kitchen gear required for your business. This scheme comes with a repayment tenure of up to 3 years.
  2. Stree Shakti Package
    This is one of the best government schemes for developing women entrepreneurs who run small businesses. If you own at least 50% of the business, you can enjoy a lower rate of interest with this loan package.
  3. Bharatiya Mahila Bank Business Loan
    This bank focuses on empowering women entrepreneurs. They provide MSME loans for women, training, and guidance to help your business thrive. They have special loan options to suit your needs.
  4. Mahila Udyam Nidhi Scheme
    This scheme for women entrepreneurs offers a financial boost to businesswomen in small-scale businesses. You can secure a loan of up to ₹10 lakhs to start or expand your venture. You get a tenure of up to 10 years to repay this MSME loan for women.
  5. Trade-Related Entrepreneurship Assistance and Development (TREAD) Scheme
    This women entrepreneur scheme in India brings training, credit, and backing to women in rural regions. The government lends financial support to non-government organisations (NGOs) that train and mentor women entrepreneurs.
  6. Mudra Yojana Scheme
    This funding for female entrepreneurs in India provides loans to small businesses without asking for collateral. You can avail of an MSME loan for women amounting to up to ₹10 lakhs.
  7. Stand-Up India Scheme
    Among the various government schemes for female entrepreneurs in India, this scheme supports businesswomen from scheduled castes (SC), scheduled tribes (ST), and women borrowers. You can get an MSME loan for women ranging from ₹10 lakhs to ₹1 crore to kick-start a new business.
  8. Dena Shakti Scheme
    This is one of the government schemes for developing women entrepreneurs that caters to women who wish to set foot in industries like agriculture, retail or manufacturing. It offers business loans up to ₹20 lakhs at an interest rate of 0.25% lower than the base rate.
  9. Udyogini Scheme
    This scheme offers government funding for female entrepreneurs having an annual income of ₹1.5 lakhs. It also offers loans for women having no capital but who wish to start a business. Under this scheme, you can get an MSME loan of up to ₹3 lakhs.
  10. Women Entrepreneurship Platform
    NITI Aayog started this flagship platform that hosts numerous workshops as well as educational events. The aim is to motivate many women to embark on their entrepreneurial journey.
  11. Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana
    This scheme for women entrepreneurs was primarily started to aid anyone who wished to start a micro/small enterprise. Under this scheme, you can obtain institutional credit of around ₹10 lakhs. However, many women have used this scheme to set up their businesses.

Explore these government schemes for developing women entrepreneurs and find the financial backing your business needs. Seize these opportunities and reach new heights as a female entrepreneur in India.
