Before finalising a loan, understanding business loan interest rates and repayment structures is crucial. It allows business owners to make informed financial decisions and ensures the loan remains manageable over time. Here’s what you need to know when applying for a ₹5 lakh business loan.

Interest Rates

Interest rates depend on multiple factors, including your credit score, business turnover, tenure, and the type of lender. Fintech providers like FlexiLoans offer competitive interest rates starting from 10.25% per annum. Loan Tenure Options

Common Mistakes to Avoid When Applying for a Business Loan

1. Incomplete Documentation

One of the most common business loan mistakes is submitting incomplete or inaccurate paperwork. Lenders require consistent and verifiable documents to assess eligibility. Ensure that all KYC, bank statements, and financial records are accurate and up-to-date.

2. Ignoring Credit Health

Your credit profile plays a crucial role in the decision-making process. A poor score or inconsistent repayment history may lead to delays or denials. Monitor your credit regularly and settle any pending dues to strengthen your credit profile before applying.

3. Overestimating Loan Requirements

Borrowing more than needed can increase the EMI burden and reduce your chances of approval. Requesting a realistic amount aligned with your business needs improves the chances of getting loan approval fast.

4. Not Comparing Lenders

Choosing the first available option without researching other lenders can result in unfavourable terms. Compare offerings from banks, NBFCs, and fintech providers, such as FlexiLoans, to find the best match.

5. Skipping Fine Print

Overlooking terms and conditions can lead to confusion about interest rates, tenure, or prepayment charges—one of the often-cited business loan rejection reasons.
