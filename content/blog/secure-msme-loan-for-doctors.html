Steps to Secure an MSME Loan Successfully

To improve your likelihood of securing an MSME loan for your clinic or hospital, adhere to these guidelines:

1. Ensure you are Registered on the Udyam Portal

To qualify for MSME loans for doctors, you must register on the Udyam portal. The Udyam portal certifies and identifies small businesses like individual clinics and doctor’s chambers. This registration serves as proof of your legal existence and also makes you eligible for government-backed collateral-free loans.

2. Check your Credit Score

You must maintain a good credit score when applying for MSME loans for doctors. Most lenders will check your credit score and history to assess your creditworthiness. If you have a credit score of 750 or above, you have a better chance of getting MSME loans approved. Check your credit score before applying to minimise the chances of rejection.

3. Choose the Right Lender

Conduct thorough research to find financial institutions that offer MSME loans for doctors. Choosing a lender that caters to medical professionals can help you access tailored benefits. Compare business loan interest rates, loan amounts, and repayment tenures from different lenders to pick the most affordable option that suits your financial goals.

4. Review the Loan Amount

You can use MSME loans for doctors without restrictions on utilising the funds. You can use these loans to expand your clinic, purchase new medical equipment, or meet working capital needs. MSME loan amounts can range from Rs. 50,000 to a few crores. However, carefully evaluate the loan amount to ensure it can adequately cover your business expenses.

5. Explore Government Initiatives

The Ministry of MSME offers several schemes to help small businesses and self-employed professionals like you in India. Look for lenders that provide MSME loans for doctors covered by schemes like the Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises, which provides credit guarantees. Loans covered by the CGTMSE eliminate the need for collateral or third-party guarantees for loans up to Rs. 5 Crores.

6. Check the Eligibility Checklist

Check the business loan eligibility criteria once you have decided on the purpose, loan amount, and lender. Lenders typically offer MSME loans for doctors with degrees like MBBS, BAMS, BUMS, BHMS, or BDS. They may also require statutory approvals from the relevant regulatory authority. Some lenders may even ask for a minimum work experience of 2 years after qualification.

7. Select an Appropriate Application Mode

While most lenders offer online and offline application modes, some public banks may require you to submit offline forms. Choose the mode that is more convenient for you. Applying online allows you to use it from the comfort of your home, while the offline mode requires visiting the bank or NBFC office.

8. Submit the Required Documents

You must accompany your completed MSME finance application form with documents supporting your eligibility. Most MSME loans for doctors require minimal documentation, such as your medical registration certificate, KYC documents (identity and address proof), bank statements, and IT returns. Since the document list may vary across lenders, formulate a checklist before applying to ensure a smooth loan processing experience and minimise the chances of rejection.
