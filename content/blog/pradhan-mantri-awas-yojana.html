What is Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)?

The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, or PMAY, is the national flagship scheme for urban housing, launched to provide affordable housing to all Indians by 2022. It was inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on the 25th of June, 2015, and was supposed to be implemented in phases over the next five years, starting with slums and unplanned cities before reaching rural areas. The project aimed to make available 20 million homes, half of them through urban slums, by March 2022.

The objective of this program is to provide affordable houses to people living in these areas. These houses will be built on various site locations such as public lands, government land (including vacant spaces), and private land.

Under PMAY, state governments have been asked to ensure that at least 10% of their houses are built using affordable housing norms. The scheme primarily aims to provide affordable housing in urban areas, where land prices are higher than average, and demand for low-cost housing is high.  

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Online Application 2021-22

We can apply for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana online using the PMAY portal in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Browse the PMAY website. You can do so by visiting this link: pmaymis.gov.in.

Step 2: On the homepage, click on the Citizen Assessment tab. A drop-down menu will appear on the screen. On this menu, click on either of the first two options, i.e., ‘For Slum Dwellers’ or ‘Benefit under the other three components’.

Step 3: You will be asked to enter our Aadhar details next. Enter the Aadhar number without any spaces and click on the check button.

Step 4: Next, you will be directed to a new page, which is the actual PMAY application form page. Here, you have to fill in all the details very carefully. Some points you need to fill in include city name, state name, contact details, bank details, income details, etc.

Step 5: Once all the details have been filled, click on the acknowledgment box. Fill in the captcha code on the screen and then click on the Save option.

Our application for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojna is now completed, and now we can take a printout of the application at this point for future references.

How to check the PMAY application status online?

Once we have successfully applied for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, we might want to check its status. The good news is we can check our Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana application status online using the assessment ID or by providing details like Father’s name and mobile number. Here is a detailed step-by-step process of how to check the PMAY application status using both methods.

1. How to check PMAY application status online using assessment ID?

Follow the given details to check the current status of our PMAY application online using assessment ID:

Step 1: Visit this link PMAY application or login to the PMAY website and visit the Citizen Assessment tab on the homepage. 

Step 2: A drop-down list will appear on the screen. From this list, click on the Check Status option.

Step 3: Now, we will see two options on our screen: By ‘Name, Father’s Name & Mobile No’ and ‘By Assessment ID’. Choose the second option, i.e., By Assessment ID.

Step 4: Next, enter the assessment ID and mobile number. The assessment ID would be allotted to us during the application submission.

Step 5: Next, click the Submit button after filling in the required details. After which, we would be able to see the current status of our application.

2. How to check PMAY application status online using details such as Father’s name & mobile number?

Follow the given details to check the current status of the PMAY application online using the following information:

Step 1: Visit PMAY application or login to the PMAY website and visit the Citizen Assessment tab on the homepage. 

Step 2: A drop-down list will appear on the screen. From this list, click on the Check Status option.

Step 3: Now, you will see two options on our screen: By ‘Name, Father’s Name & Mobile No’ and ‘By Assessment ID’. Choose the ‘By Name, Father’s Name & Mobile No’ option.

Step 4: Next, you will be asked to provide other details, such as state name, district name, father’s name, ID type, and Aadhar number.

Step 5: After filling in all the details, click on the Submit option. Now, you will be able to track down the status of your PMAY application.
