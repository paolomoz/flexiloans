Small Business Loans, Business Credit Cards or Corporate Credit: Which is Best For Your Business

Sometimes there are instances in the business when you have a lack of funds. You may urgently require a particular amount of money. This could be for the implementation of a business strategy or any such reasons. However, when your business lacks funds, you can opt from small business loans, business credit cards, or corporate credit.

Posted Jan 16, 2021 · Updated Jul 25, 2025 · 5 min read

It may be quite confusing for a company when it comes to choosing the best amongst these three. This selection is completely based on the condition as well as a requirement for your business. Let us first discuss each one of them for a better understanding.

Small Business Loans:

A company goes for this when they require a good amount of money. It could be for any business purpose like meeting the requirements of current debt, or to make a big investment. When taking a business loan, a company must plan the amount for the business loan by considering the interest rates and the periods of repayment. These business loans can also be in the form of invoice financing.

But the bank or the lender may not easily approve a business loan. When the amount of loan is higher, then the probability of its approval depends on the revenue generated by your company. One of the major benefits of business loan is you do not have to pay higher interest rates for a business line of credit.

For instance, your company came up with a business strategy. However, you are not able to implement and give it a start due to a lack of money. If you feel confident that your strategy will generate a good amount of revenue, then you must go for a business loan. Even if you are unable to pay an existing debt to a lender, then take the benefits of business loan and manage the same. Invoice financing is also one of the benefits of small business loan.

Business Credit Cards:

Each company has some of the other kinds of expenses that they need to meet for a well functioning business. But when these tasks are getting delayed due to a lack of funds, then you can use a business credit card for a working capital loan. The interest charged only on the amount you have spent. Also, based on your expenses and needs of the merchant cash advance, you can set its credit limit.

As compared to business loans, these credit cards are quite easily approved by a bank or lender. But a business credit card is not an option if you require a large sum of money. When you regularly use it, you may get some sort of rewards, offers, or cashback. At the end of the month, when the bill is sent for a business line of credit, you can easily track and look at the overall expenses made by the company.

When you want a merchant cash advance, you do not have to give any security money or any kind of assets before starting to use it. Apart from that, based on the activities and repayment regularity of the credit card bills, your credit score is maintained. If you require a business loan in the future, then you can easily get it, assuming that you have well maintained your credit score.

Corporate Credits:

Similar to credits offered to individuals, corporate credits are for a particular company or business. The reason for opting for a business line of credit can be anything. When you establish a corporate credit and maintain it properly, then your profile will leave a good impression in front of the clients. This contributes to the growth of small businesses.

There are some firms that rate your corporate credits. This means each time when your payment to a company or a business is done on time; then, your corporate credit rating gets improved. Corporate credits are also a kind of working capital loan. These types of loans can be taken to pay previous debts or hire additional and well-experienced staff.

For example, if there is more demand for goods in the market and you need some funds to increase the production of goods. But when a company lacks this amount, they often opt for corporate credits for a business line of credit. When you have generated good revenue, you can pay back the credit amount. The interest rates on these loans are based on the rating of your corporate credit profile.

Conclusion:

Now we are well aware of what is a small business loan, business credit cards, and corporate credit. Based on your requirements and the amount, you can choose one of them wisely. There are also some benefits of a business loan that you will enjoy opting for any of these. You can grow your business when you are able to get the required finances on time.

Are you still feeling confused and are unable to make the decision? Then go through two main factors listed below that must be considered choosing one from these three.

  1. Based on Reasons/ Needs:

What is the reason you want business credit? Most of the time, it is either for a big investment or implementation of a business strategy, or it could be a working capital loan.

If you want to work on a business strategy or want finances for investment, then you must go with small business loans. While if you require money to meek your business expenses on a monthly basis, then look for business credit cards, a merchant cash advance, or corporate credit.

  1. Based on the Amount:

When the amount of money that you require is large, then your financial needs will be satisfied with small business loans. But if you need a decent amount of money on a monthly basis to cover all the basic business or company expenses, that is working capital loan; then you can have business credit cards, a merchant cash advance, invoice financing, or corporate credit.
