Eligibility Criteria to Get a ₹15 Lakh Loan

To qualify for a ₹15 lakh business loan, lenders typically require that your business or trade has been operational for at least one year. It must also generate consistent monthly revenue and maintain a positive credit history. These factors help assess your financial reliability and repayment capacity.

Business Loan Criteria 2025

It’s necessary to make sure your company fulfils the requirements prior to applying for a ₹15 lakh business loan. Although eligibility criteria for MSME loan vary from lender to lender, this lists the common requirements for collateral-free business loans qualification requirements:

  • Business Vintage: Your trade should be at least 1 year old. Having a stable operating history increases your chances of business loan approval.
  • Monthly Turnover: Your business must generate a minimum monthly turnover of ₹1.5 to ₹2 lakh. This shows that your business generates a steady income, which makes you fit for repayment.
  • CIBIL Score: A good credit score of 700+ generally helps you qualify for a business loan and get lower interest rates and favourable terms.
  • Business Registration: You can significantly increase your chances of approval by registering your business on Udyam and for GST.
  • Applicant Age: At the time of applying for ₹15 lakh business loan, you, as a business owner and applicant, must be between 21 to 65 years old.

Eligibility for MSME loans helps lenders determine your repayment capacity. If you meet the eligibility criteria, you are more likely to get a loan fast and simply. It also strengthens your application and gets you a low interest business loan.
