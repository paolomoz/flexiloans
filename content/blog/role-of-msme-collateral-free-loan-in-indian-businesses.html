What is MSME Business Loan?

MSMEs can get collateral-free loans in India for business setups or expand the already existing ones. They get charged a minor ROI (8 to 16%) for such loans. Each bank has different policies on deciding the maximum loan amount considering their norms. However, many organizations do not set a limit on the maximum loan amount. The term to repay these loans can be up to 15 years.

The financial institutes, such as NBFCs and Banks, offer loans to MSMEs. In addition to that, the Government of India keeps launching new schemes to support MSMEs so they can grow more. The Government charges a minimal rate of interest on such loans. Several schemes that provide collateral-free loans to MSMEs include CGTMSE, Mudra loan, etc. Let us get detailed information about the business loan.

Is MSME Loan a Business Loan Without Collateral?

MSME loans are both collateral or collateral-free. Though, many financial institutes offer loans without any security. Hence these are called collateral-free loans. In collateral-free loans, the lenders do not ask to submit any security with the financial institutions or banks.

These MSME loans have numerous features as below:

  • Business loans or collateral-free loans with up to INR 1 crore.
  • Such business loans (collateral free) are short-term, and the borrowers can repay the amount within a tenure of one year (Monthly) and a maximum of up to 5 years.
  • The MSME loans are offered by the financial institutions, which include NBFCs, Public Sector Banks, Rural Banks, SFBs, and MFIs, at very reasonable ROI.
  • The amount of the loan depends upon the profile of the applicant and their credit score, repayment capability, financial stability, and creditworthiness.

These loans get disbursed rapidly by the banks of NBFC. Usually, it does not take more than a week. Several lenders hardly take a day or two for disbursal of the loan amount.
