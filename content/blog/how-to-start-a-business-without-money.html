How To Start a Business Without Money

No matter how great a business idea is, everyone needs money to make it happen. But what should someone do when they are broke? How do they get the funding they need to get their business off the ground?

Posted Sep 2, 2022 · Updated Apr 28, 2026 · 5 min read

Preparatory steps for starting a business even with no finance

Plenty of entrepreneurs have started businesses with little to no money. This article will show how to start a business with no money. It will cover everything from coming up with a great business idea to finding funding and making the first sale.

Let’s get started!

Step 1: Find a profitable business idea

The first step in starting a business with no money is finding a profitable business idea. And there are a few ways to go about doing this. One option is to look for problems that need to be solved in the community. What do people need that they can’t find? Is there a gap in the market that could be filled? Another option is to think about what you are passionate about. What do you love doing? What are you good at? This can be a great way to develop business ideas that can likely stick in the long run. Once you develop a few ideas, it’s time to do market research. This will help determine whether there’s a demand for a product or service. There are several ways to go about this. First, one could start by conducting online searches and reading industry reports, or they could directly reach out to the target market and ask them what they think.

Step 2: Create a lean business plan

The next step is to create a lean business plan. This is a stripped-down version of a traditional business plan focused on the essentials.

Start by creating a one-page business plan that includes the following:

  •  business idea
  • target market
  •  unique selling proposition (USP)
  • revenue model
  • costs
  • marketing strategy
  • milestones

This will make it easier to adjust and go and avoid getting bogged down in the details.

Step 3: Look for alternative sources of funding

Just because someone is broke doesn’t mean they can’t get funding for their business. There are some alternative sources of funding that they can tap into. One option is to look for grants or competitions they can enter when starting a business without money. This can be a great way to get seed money to start a business. Another option when starting a business without money is to crowdfund the business on platforms like Kickstarter or Indiegogo. This is where they raise money from a large group of people in exchange for rewards. They should also consider taking out a personal loan or using a credit card to finance the business. Just be sure to carefully consider the interest rates and terms before they commit to anything.

Step 4: Build a product or service

If someone is unsure how to get started, several resources can help, like books, online courses, and tutorials. They could also reach out to mentors or experts in the field for advice. Once they have a prototype of their product or service, it’s time to start testing it. This will help them make sure it’s ready for launch.

Step 6: Launch the business

Once someone has tested their product or service and made the necessary changes, it’s time to launch the business with no money. There are many ways one can go about this. They could launch an online store, sell through distributors, or set up a brick-and-mortar location. They could also consider partnering with other businesses to help get the product or service in front of more people.

Step 7: Market the business

This is where business owners can let people know about their product or service and why they can buy it. There are some ways to go about this, such as creating a website, running ads, or doing public relations. They could also consider partnering with other businesses to help get the product or service in front of more people. Once one has started marketing the business, it’s time to start growing it.
