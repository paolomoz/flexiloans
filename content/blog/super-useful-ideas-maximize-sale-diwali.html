BUSINESS IDEAS FOR DIWALI TO MAXIMIZE YOUR SALES

Posted Aug 14, 2017 · Updated Sep 10, 2020 · 3 min read

Diwali is a festival of lights and every business is raking up its operations to meet the high demand around this time of the year. Though good sales volume is guaranteed due to the nature of this festival, is your business really “Diwali-ready”? Do you have enough funds to keep the business going on without glitches? To keep the financial troubles at bay during the festival of lights, you should consider borrowing small business loans.

Here are some useful ideas to maximize your sale and get ready to take on the high season with small business loans.

SCALE UP YOUR BUSINESS THIS DIWALI

It goes without saying that the production shall have to be at its peak to meet the Diwali demand which will lead to either expansion of existing capacities or utilize existing production capacity to its fullest. You can make use of collateral free loans for business expansion for this purpose. They are short term loans for which you don’t need to mortgage any asset and can avail quickly without undergoing a cumbersome process.

BUY/RENT EQUIPMENT AND REFURBISH THE STORE

The scaling up of operations needs additional equipment. You may need to spend money to buy or rent it. You may also require to refurbish the store fronts and upgrade the interiors to make them visually appealing to entice the customers. Of course the equipment installation, renovations, upgrades, and infrastructure can prove to be expensive, but collateral free loans can fulfill the financial need easily.

STOCK UP THE INVENTORY

To meet the demand, production needs to begin months before which means maintaining high raw material as well as finished product stocks. This holds true for traders and shopkeepers too. High stock levels translate into fund crunch as there is a cost of holding and production, but the revenue generation is still a while away. This gap can be bridged with the help of collateral free business loans which can be borrowed without any hassles or placing a collateral.

CREATE A MARKETING BUZZ

In order to attract more customers into your store, you need to increase the visibility of your business. This calls for allocating a major portion of your Diwali budget to marketing. You will also need to hire additional staff and order gifts for loyal customers. All such heightened business activities come with a hefty price tag, but working capital loans can take care of it all. Working Capitals are nothing but a category of small business loans specifically designed for business enterprises.

DIWALI OFFERS, DISCOUNTS AND SALE

It is common for businesses to offer attractive discounts, deals, and sale during Diwali season. Small business loans can help you in running these lucrative schemes without putting an additional burden on your finances.

This Diwali take your small business to big heights with unsecured Business loans that ensure that your operations are well funded while you take advantage of the high Diwali demand!
