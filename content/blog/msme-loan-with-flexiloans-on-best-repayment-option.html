MSMEs are micro, small, and medium enterprises that either manufacture or process goods or are involved in the services sector. They play a major role in India’s economy. They encourage self-employment and employment opportunities (skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled people) and contribute significantly to India’s GDP (Gross Domestic Product) by about 27 percent. The Indian government plans to increase the share of the MSMEs to 50 percent in the future.
