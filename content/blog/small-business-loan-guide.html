HERE IS A STEP-WISE GUIDE TO SMALL BUSINESS LOAN IN INDIA

Before applying for a loan, you need to figure out the exact reason. The reason for which you need to avail finances will drive your choice of loan type. So, take time to ascertain the need for financing. Here are some categories of business loan types:

LINE OF CREDIT

The line of credit financing works like a credit card for your small business. Under this credit facility, the financier issues a pool of fund for your business. Depending on the seasonal requirements of your business you can withdraw the funds. And interest would only be levied on the money withdrawn. Soon after you pay back the borrowed amount along with its interest you are again entitled to use those funds. Thus, the line of credit business loan is a viable choice for businesses that have fluctuating capital requirements.

MERCHANT CASH ADVANCE

If part of your business revenue is generated from credit or debit card payments, then a merchant cash advance is the best funding option for your business needs. Based upon the monthly sales volume the lender purchases a part of your credit card sales. Repayments under this funding are made by extracting a proportion of your revenue obtained through credit card sales. Many SMEs find procuring merchant cash advance an easy funding option as under this the repayments can be made on a daily basis without any hassle of hefty monthly EMIs.

WORKING CAPITAL LOANS

A working capital loan intends to meet the everyday demands of your business. These are short term loans that cover operational needs. These loans can be used for financing offline as well as online businesses. The working capital loans are appropriate solutions for the ventures that face the short term cash flow issues.

WHAT SHOULD YOU CONSIDER BEFORE APPLYING FOR A BUSINESS LOAN?

Before heading to apply for a small business loan in India you need to consider the following factors. Based on these your lender would evaluate you and your business performance:

CREDIT SCORE

A good personal and business credit score plays an important role in acquiring a business loan. However, a bad credit score will not necessarily rule you out but it can impact the terms of your loan. It can affect the interest rates levied. While contrary to this a good creditworthiness will help you fetch loan faster and sooner.

CASH FLOW

Cash flow is one of the focal indicators of your business success. It provides the lender information about your financial capacity of paying back the loan. The stronger is the business’s income and cash flow are the more easily you are likely to obtain the funding.

BUSINESS PLAN

Most of the lenders look for an effective business plan to disburse the finances. Your business must present a clear picture about every aspect and should be sufficiently viable to secure funding.

BUSINESS TENURE

Getting a business loan is much easier with longer tenure. Based on your experience in business the lender evaluates the consistency of your business. Thus, longer is your track record easier would be the financing.

STANDARD DOCUMENTS NEEDED TO APPLY FOR A SMALL BUSINESS LOAN IN INDIA

Applying for a business loan will require you to submit specific information about you and your business. The list of documents that would be required to validate the information about you and your business include:

  • Identity proof
  • Residential address proof
  • PAN cards of the business and other operating personnel
  • Certificate of Incorporation or the Partnership Deed
  • Financial statements of at least the last three years, including Profit & Loss Accounts, Audited Balance Sheets, and Reports.
  • Personal & Business Tax Returns for the last 3 years.
  • Last 6 months bank account statement (business and self)
