Overview- Who is a Dealer, and What is a Dealership Business?

One of the easiest and most lucrative businesses to start in India today is the dealership business. 

It is a fact that dealership in India has evolved significantly over the past few years. Numerous small-scale industrialists and merchants got specific dealerships and established their business after getting all the aspects right, that is, by extensive planning and implementing effective strategies. 

The dealership is a business involved in the authorization of selling or distributing specific products in a particular area. Being a dealer, you are responsible for purchasing the products or goods directly from manufacturers and selling them to your clients or even the consumer.

A dealer primarily acts as a middleman between the manufacturer, distributors, and consumers. They are involved in trading a particular product or item. But why are numerous individuals opting to start and get dealership businesses? Let’s find out below!

Why Become a Dealer?

The products or items dealers buy from producers are sold to supermarkets, online retailers, or consumers. The dealers are engaged in commercial trade as part of their business for their benefit. The best thing about being a dealer is that they are not required to block a hefty initial investment to buy and stock the products in a warehouse.  

How to Start Dealership Business?

Wondering what’s the procedure to acquire the license for dealership products and begin your journey as a dealer? We will help you to know the crucial steps in understanding How to take dealership of any company.

1. Make a Compelling Business Strategy

Developing a competent and influential business strategy will help you define your dealership business, give it an adequate set of values, and draft a purpose for your dealership business.  

Preparing a potent strategy after conducting thorough research will provide a roadmap for your upcoming dealership business.  

The key areas you must determine while crafting a dealership business strategy include your budget, financial strategy, management, and operational plans. Once you have ensured what type of dealership you want to enter, prepare further plans accordingly. 

2. Select a Product

The foremost step is to choose the product you want to sell via the online or offline store. But how do you opt for the right product that will streamline the process of establishing a company? Besides capitalising on early trends within your neighbourhood, and considering your professional experience, try to opt for a product that solves the customer pain points. 

Talking to other local dealers to get an idea about what all products are high in demand will also help. Make a list of items, and conduct a litmus test for those products, which will determine the apt product you can sell.

3. Find Your Dealer

The next crucial step is to determine the dealer or manufacturer from whom you will purchase the product. Before choosing a dealer, always examine their legal documents and verify their commercial operations. It is also important to verify their work permit and licence for security operations. 

4. Establish a Workplace

While signing the dealership agreement after outlining all the terms and conditions of the business, the next step is to set up a workplace or a shop in the local area. This place can further be used to stock the inventory. However, in the initial stages of the business, you can also save funds by doing it from home.  

5. Find The Franchisor

Things become more manageable if you own a franchise, as it will let you skip a startup page, instant name recognition, and boost the probability of your success. 

6. Create a Robust Network

So, how does the best dealership business in India work? They connect with potential suppliers, dealers, and distributors and maximise the scope of their earnings by building a strong network. 

7. Have a Purchase and Credit Policy

Buying the products in bulk, getting lump sum discounts, and then packing them in smaller quantities to yield higher profits is the idea behind establishing the purchase policy. 

Another dynamic aspect of the dealership business is to have a firm credit policy by checking the list of your buyers and ensuring whether they can buy the products from you after validating their credentials. 

Best Dealership Business in India

Here are some of the best dealership business ideas to look at:

1. Healthcare and Beauty & Wellness Dealership Business

Considered the best distributorship business in India, the healthcare and beauty sector is one of the biggest and fastest-developing sectors, consuming more than 12% of GDP in most countries. The business dealership can thrive by showcasing well-known brands in healthcare, personal care items, particular brands, cosmetics, etc. You can also establish a home-based healthcare and beauty & wellness company with minimal investment. 

2. Organic Food Dealership Business

People are becoming more health conscious, and the consumer demand for organic food shows double-digit growth. Therefore, one can start a dealership business in Organic food, especially in metro cities, and earn massively. 

3. Automobile Dealership Business

One of the most thriving sectors, the Automobile industry, is consistently booming. One can start by opening a small retail store that deals in selling different automobile accessories and parts. In addition, offering other services like car washing to the customers might turn out to be more profitable. 

You may consider opting for a reputed automobile dealership in India, such as Maruti Suzuki, Hero Moto Corp, Bajaj, TVS, etc., which will ensure increased sales, and reap ample profits.
