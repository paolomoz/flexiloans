Customer Retention Rate

Customer retention rate is a crucial indicator of their capacity to sustain a steady customer base over an extended period of time for MSMEs. Long-term business success depends on customer satisfaction and loyalty.

Formula for calculating customer retention rate:


Customer Retention Rate = ((Number of Customers at End of Period – Number of New Customers Acquired During Period) / Number of Customers at Start of Period) x 100
