Difference Between Personal Loan and Business Loan

As the name suggests, personal loan is usually acquired or granted for personal reasons like medical expenses, tuition fees, travelling and also business.

A business loan, on the other hand, is acquired or granted for the sole reason of covering various business expenses. It includes meeting essential business needs, buying equipment, and managing inventory, among others.

What Makes Business Loan Right for Your Business?

No matter the industry your business operates in, you will always need funds to expand or meet existing commitments. What most businessmen don’t realise is that taking a loan is itself a commitment and comes with monetary repayment.

Benefits of Taking a Business Loan

You can obtain a Business Loan from different banks or NBFCs for reasons ranging from business expansion to taking advantage of new business opportunities. When you apply for a business loan, you get access to the following business loan benefits:

  • Flexible Loan Amount: Business loans offer greater flexibility in loan amounts. This allows you to support greater business requirements such as growth plans, inventory acquisitions, and operating demands.
  • Business-specific Customisation: Business loans are solely obtained to meet the business demands. These demands include managing cash flow, business expansion, seasonal business requirements, and working capital requirements.
  • Improves Business Credit: Prompt loan repayment improves your business credit and facilitates future borrowing. It helps you get better loan terms for your business, including reduced interest rates and longer payback periods.
  • Tax Benefits: Your business has numerous tax obligations that take a big chunk of revenue. When you avail a business loan, it helps you reduce your taxable income and lowers your overall tax liability.

Why are Business Loans Better for Business Growth?

The growth of every business depends greatly on the investments you make in your business. Whether you are stocking up on seasonal products or improving infrastructure, every investment leads you closer to your goals. At times, this may require you to get a higher loan amount, which a personal loan does not offer.

It is just not the higher loan amount where the business loan proves to be better than personal loans. Here are the key reasons why business loans are better for business growth:

  • Larger Funding: As previously stated, you need larger funding to meet varying business demands and long-term investments. This will promote you to keep your business one step ahead of your rivals.
  • Structured Repayment Plans: Business loans with structured payback plans help you manage your debt and preserve financial stability. This not only helps you maintain cash flow but also avoids defaults.
  • Build Strong Business Credit Profile: Taking a business loan builds a strong business credit profile, which is essential to business expansion and financial stability. The profile makes it simple for you to get favorable financing arrangements.

Tips to Decide the Right Loan for Your Business

Follow these few important tips in order to select the right business loan:

  • Assess Your Funding Needs: First, you must determine how much money you need for your business. Calculate the exact amount of funding you need by considering financial requirements.
  • Evaluate Your Eligibility: Check your personal and business credit scores to learn more about your financial situation and eligibility. After evaluating your eligibility, gather the necessary paperwork to back up your loan application.
  • Compare Interest Rates, Repayment Terms, and Flexibility: To get the finest loan terms, compare the interest rates offered by different lenders. Then, review the repayment terms to ensure they fit your company’s financial flow.
  • Consider Future Business Growth and Credit Requirements: Know if the loan meets your business’s current and future finance requirements. Think about how the loan will impact your credit score and ability to borrow in the future.

Business Loan vs Personal Loan: Right Choice for You

There is a clear choice when it comes to choosing between the business loan vs personal loan. This choice, however, relies greatly upon your business needs, objectives and goals.

1. Personal Loan

A personal loan is the right choice for you if you need money quickly to meet urgent business demands. However, there is a lesser flexibility in terms of amount and tenure.

2. Business Loan

Assume that you need a lump sum for your business that is customised to your business. If that is so, a business loan is the right choice for you. You have access to not only flexible loan amounts but also tax benefits. An additional benefit to all this is that it helps you improve your business credit.

The right choice lies in your financial needs and the associated circumstances. In either situation, you must first understand your financial needs and the terms of the loan.
