Best Food Startups Ideas to Try in 2021

Here are some of the innovative food business ideas:

Restaurant/Cafe/Coffee Shop

Restaurants and cafes are the most secure options for any F&B (food and beverage) startup. It requires extensive and precise preparation, such as choosing the location, arranging supplier logistics, engaging high-quality professional chefs, etc. Once these are in place, restaurants can earn a lot of money with no/minimal inputs other than daily maintenance.

With the startup wave enveloping the country, the restaurant sector has been significantly impacted. It has inspired many people to establish their enterprises. Both experts and home cooks are now channeling their passion for food by entering the restaurant business.

You need between Rs. 15 and 20 lakh to open a café. And if you provide a stylish dining environment and high-quality cuisine, you may attract locals, office workers, and students. The best part is that you may receive a business loan for coffee shop of up to Rs. 7 lakhs to slowly and gradually develop and establish your business.

Bakery

Everyone enjoys a delicious cake or pastry. Setting up a bakery may be a very profitable business idea if done correctly. Bakeries can also distribute baked products via online orders, social media, or web portals, which takes little expenditure. This means you make huge profits with little to no investment other than the expense of establishing a business. You can apply for a business loan for a bakery.

Catering

If you believe your management and people skills are suited to F&B services, you may offer a wide range of catering services, which might help you earn significant returns on nearly every customer added. Catering also boosts the overall business exposure and helps establish commercial relationships.

Catering in India is now an essential element for the success of events and social gatherings. It is one of the fastest-growing industries, with demand rising across the country.

Food Trucks

Pop-up restaurants have grown in popularity in recent years, helping deliver a range of regional fast foods and fancy cuisines worldwide. In line with this trend, fast food trucks are quickly expanding in India, particularly in urban areas such as Delhi, Bangalore, and Mumbai. This is due to the inexpensive nature of food trucks, which means lower starting costs, easy maintenance, need for fewer employees, and suitable hands-on operations.

India is a late starter in the food truck industry, but we are catching up. There is a vast potential in this kind of business because public demands change with time.

The food truck industry essentially delivers faster, provides healthier food, and is far less expensive than other eateries. It is one of the upcoming trends, and if done correctly, it may lead to significant commercial success. Food truck licenses in Mumbai can be obtained online from the relevant authority.

Homemade Food Business

This might help you make money rapidly. Today, a trend of selling homemade meals to consumers has emerged due to one’s love and enthusiasm for food. Nothing beats a homemade meal service if you can invest between Rs. 50,000 and 1 lakh and establish a food business. All you need is an online MSME registration, GST registration, and a food safety license.

Ice Cream Shop

We all scream for ice cream. Everyone enjoys ice cream, and also the brain freeze that results from eating it too quickly. Opening an ice cream store is a highly realistic choice since it attracts consumers from around the city and at nearly any time of the day or night. Every child’s favourite food is ice cream, and because summer lasts the longest in India, ice cream is perhaps the most profitable business.

The ice cream industry has experienced rapid innovation in recent years. Previously, wayside carts were popular, and children and adults frequented them. Ice cream parlours, where individuals may eat ice cream at leisure, are becoming increasingly popular these days. An ice cream parlour license is mandatory.

Juice Shop/Milkshakes

Juices and milkshakes are staples of a morning diet and during the summers. A juice store may perform exceptionally well in generating income with very little expenditure. Purchasing ingredients such as fruits and milk to sell high-quality organic juices and milkshakes at a favorable site requires little expenditure

Farsan/Namkeen Shop

Making farsan/namkeen at home is time-consuming for most individuals. When inviting guests for parties and welcoming them to their homes, people prefer to buy these food products from shops that sell their favourite namkeen or farsan. Setting up a farsan/namkeen shop can significantly increase revenues for a food business.

Sweets Shop

In India, it is customary to offer sweets to relatives when visiting them or meeting friends after a long time. People frequently buy sweets on the move and in large quantities. Thus, establishing a business selling sweetmeats at a well-planned site can generate significant profits with minimal effort on the owner’s part.

Dairy Shop

Milk and milk products, such as paneer and cheese, are essential foods in India. Therefore, a dairy would never go out of business. Whether you are storing items from other dairy aggregators such as Amul or supporting local/self-made dairy products, it is safe to assume that these stores will attract both existing and new customers every day and at all times of the day.
