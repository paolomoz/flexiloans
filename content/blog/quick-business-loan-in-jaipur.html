Types of Business Loans Available in Jaipur

The following are the various forms of business loans accessible to you:

Short-term loan

In the event of a term loan, a borrower can choose between a secured and an unsecured business loan to obtain cash based on the status and needs of the firm. These loans can be used to purchase long-term investments. Term loans are classified into three forms depending on the tenure: short-term loans, long-term loans, and intermediate loans, which can be returned monthly or quarterly. While the interest rate on such loans might be fixed or floating, it changes depending on the loan payback term.

Letter of credit facility

A firm can get this financing plan based on the creditworthiness of the buyer’s bank when the buyer and seller do not know each other, such as during international trade transactions. In this scenario, the bank promises to pay the seller on time after considering its origin certificate, insurance certificate, transportation papers, legal documents, and other business documents. If the buyer fails to pay, the bank is obligated to pay the total outstanding sum.

Credit facility in cash

A cash credit facility is an overdraft loan that a firm can use to finance its working capital needs by pledging current assets such as receivables, inventories, and so on as security. The bank’s stock margin determines the maximum amount that may be withdrawn under this system. This loan can be renewed at regular intervals of 12 months.

Bank guarantee

You can obtain this pre-approved secured loan by submitting residential, commercial, or industrial property as collateral if you own a public/private limited corporation or a proprietorship/partnership firm. This funding system assists businesses in obtaining loans, purchasing necessary equipment, or covering any additional charges that will help the organisation flourish. In this type of business loan, the financing firm pledges to compensate the guaranteed business’s stakeholders if it defaults.

Equipment financing
