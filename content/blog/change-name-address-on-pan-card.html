How To Update Pan Card Online

Here is  how you can update PAN card details:

Step 1: You need to go to www.tin-nsdl.com, the official NSDL E-Governance portal.

Step 2: In the Services section, select “PAN.”

Step 3: In the “Correction/Change in PAN Data” box, press “Apply.”

Step 4: Click ‘Changes or Corrections in Existing PAN Data/Reprint of PAN Card (No Changes in Existing PAN Data)’ seen from the ‘Application Type’ dropdown box.

Step 5: Pick the relevant classification of the assessee from the ‘Category’ dropdown option. For example, if the PAN is issued in your name, choose ‘Individual’ from the list.

Step 6: Now input your name, email address, birth date, and phone number.

Step 7: Enter the Captcha and click “Submit.”

Step 8: Your application will be recorded, and a Token Code will be emailed to the email address you supplied. You can proceed with the procedure by clicking a button underneath it.

Step 9: After you continue, you will be routed to the form – there are three alternatives for submitting your papers, choose “Submit scanned pictures using e-Sign on NSDL e-gov.”

Step 10: Enter all of the required information, including your father’s name, mother’s name (not mandatory), and Aadhaar card number, and then select ‘Next.’

Step 11:You will now be directed to a new webpage where you may update your correct address.

Step 12:Upload any necessary documents, such as proof of address, age, and identity.

Step 13: Confirm the declaration and press the “Submit” button.

Step 14: You’ll be sent to the payment page. Payment methods include net banking, credit/debit card and demand draft.

Step 15: An acknowledgment slip will be created upon successful payment. The candidate should print it out and deliver it together with the physical verification of papers to the NSDL e-gov office. Attach two images in the area provided, as well as a sign across it. On the front of the letter, write ‘Application for PAN Change’ and the acknowledgement number.

Pan Card Name Change Process

You can change name in PAN card as per Aadhaar number in the following ways:

Offline application for change of name:

  • The PAN card form correction download link is available here for offline applications.
  • The PAN card Form must be completed accurately, with all facts provided.
  • Before filling out the form, the photograph must be attached and the form must be duly signed.
  • In the event of an offline application, a demand draft in the name of NSDL must be provided with the application form to the NSDL’s registered address.

This is most likely the simplest approach to amend your PAN card’s name online. 

Online application is made in the following manner:

  • The online application may be made through TIN-NSDL or UTIITSL.
  • Payment for the online application form must be paid online using a debit/credit card or online banking.
  • A 15-digit acknowledgment number is created, which may be used to check the status of a PAN card.
  • The applicant obtains the amended PAN card within 45 days of filing the application if everything is done correctly.

How to Change Address on PAN Card?

If you’ve relocated, it’s crucial to update your address on your PAN card. Here’s how:

  1. Follow the Same Process: Visit the NSDL portal, select the ‘Services’ tab, and choose ‘Change/Correction in PAN Data’.
  2. Enter New Address Information: Fill in your updated address details in the relevant section.
  3. Upload Address Proof: Upload address proof such as an Aadhaar card or utility bill.
  4. Submit Application and Payment: Complete the process, including the payment.
  5. Track Your Application: Use your acknowledgment number to track the status of your online PAN Application.

Offline Procedure

Offline procedure: To make offline adjustments, reissue, or surrender the PAN, a person must file the PAN rectification/correction form to the closest NSDL collecting center and also submit a note with the jurisdictional Assessing Officer. This form’s fields are comparable to those on the online form.

Take notice of the following:

• The legally signed acknowledgment, along with the necessary papers, must be received by the NSDL within 15 days of submission of the application online.

• Because the PAN rectification form is used for numerous purposes, including PAN rectification, reissuance of PAN without rectification, and surrender of extra PAN, it must be thoroughly filled out.

• The checkbox on the upper left of the related fields should be correctly marked and selected since selecting the wrong box can cause you further trouble. For example, if you file for PAN reissuance with no corrections and tick the box next to your name. or choose the relinquished PAN choice, your application category will be different than what you want to submit.

How to Track the Status of a PAN Card

It is simple and quick to track status of a PAN card request. When you apply for a PAN card or update/correct errors in your PAN, you will be issued an acknowledgment code that you may use to check on the status of your new PAN request or PAN Card rectification status.
