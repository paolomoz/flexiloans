Glossary: Key Terms Explained

Term
Definition
MSME
Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises—businesses classified based on investment and turnover criteria.
Working Capital Loan
A loan designed to fund day-to-day business operations, including inventory, payroll, and operational expenses.
Seasonal Business Loan
Financing tailored to help businesses manage cash flow fluctuations due to seasonal demand variations.
Collateral-Free Loan
A business loan that does not require assets like property or machinery to be pledged as security.
Equipment Financing
A loan used specifically to purchase machinery, tools, or technology to improve business operations.
Business Expansion Loan
Funding used to support geographic growth, new branch openings, or entry into new markets.
Cash Flow Management
The process of tracking and optimising the movement of cash in and out of a business to maintain liquidity.
Loan Disbursement
The process of transferring approved loan funds to the borrower’s account.
Credit History
A record of a borrower’s past borrowing and repayment behaviour, used by lenders to assess creditworthiness.
Repayment Tenure
The duration over which a loan is repaid, typically measured in months or years.
