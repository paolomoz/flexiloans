What Is an Unsecured Business Loan?

These types don’t require you to provide collateral, such as property or machinery, to secure funds. Unlike secured loans, unsecured loans allow you to access the funds based on your creditworthiness and performance.

Eligibility Criteria for FlexiLoans’ Unsecured Business Loans

  1. Your business should be operational for at least 12 months to qualify. This shows stability and assures us of your repayment ability.
  2. A minimum annual turnover should be ₹10 lakhs. This confirms your business generates enough revenue to cover repayments.
  3. A good credit score confirms your creditworthiness. A strong credit history is crucial for securing favorable loan terms.
  4. You must have a business bank account in your company’s name for disbursement and repayments.

Documents Required for Business Loan Application

  1. KYC Documents
    Provide your Aadhaar card, PAN card, passport or voter ID for identity verification.
  2. Business Registration Proof
    Submit proof of your business registration, such as a GST certificate, Shop Act License, or incorporation certificate.
  3. Bank Statements
    Include six months’ bank statements to assess your business’s financial health and cash flow.
  4. Financial Statements
    Provide profit and loss statements and balance sheets to help evaluate your business’s performance.
  5. Tax Returns
    Submit your most recent income tax returns to verify your business’s income and tax compliance.
