Dos and Don’ts For a Safe Financial Experience

When I’m looking to take out my first loan, I want to ensure that the experience is as smooth as possible.

Posted Jul 21, 2022 · Updated Jun 24, 2025 · 1 min read

I’m following these tips from Flexiloans for a safe financial experience:

Dos

  • Ensure I make payments in an “Epimoney private limited” registered bank account only.
  • Report suspected activities in my account asap.
  • Double-check the spelling of the website and make sure it’s authentic
  • Write and sign the cheque myself before handing it over to the authorized personnel.

Don’ts

  • Avoid making payments into individual accounts.
  • Do not share details with unverified persons/ accounts.
  • Do not open unknown emails and unverified applications.
  • Do not make any advance payments for loan processing.
