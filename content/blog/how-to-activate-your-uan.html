The Universal Account Number is a crucial identification number allotted by the Employee Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) to all employees contributing to the Employees Provident Fund (EPF) scheme in India. UAN activation online is a simple process that enables you to access various EPFO services, like checking your PF balance, updating your KYC details and transferring your PF account when changing jobs. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of how to activate UAN and explain its importance for your financial well-being.

Why is UAN Activation Important?

  1. Seamless PF Account Management: You can easily access your PF account details, view your balance and download your PF passbook online.
  2. Simplified Job Changes: You can easily transfer your PF account when changing jobs, eliminating the need for a new PF account creation.
  3. Online Claims and Withdrawals: It allows you to file online claims for partial withdrawals, final settlements and pension benefits.
  4. Updating KYC Details: You can update your personal information, such as your address, mobile number and email ID, through the EPFO portal.

How to Activate UAN?

  1. Visit the official EPFO website at https://unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in/memberinterface/.
  2. Click on the “Activate UAN” option under the “Manage” section.
  3. Enter your Universal Account Number, Member ID (PF account number), mobile number and captcha code.
  4. Click on the “Get Authorisation Pin” button. You will receive an OTP on your registered mobile number.
  5. Enter the OTP and click on the “Validate OTP and Activate UAN” button.
  6. You will receive a confirmation message post activation.

Factors to Consider for UAN Activation

  1. Accurate Information: Ensure that your personal details, such as your name, date of birth and mobile number, match the information provided in your EPF account. Mismatched details may delay or prevent activation.
  2. Registered Mobile Number: A valid mobile number is essential for activation. You’ll receive an OTP (One-Time Password) on this number to verify your identity during the activation process.
  3. Aadhaar / PAN Linking: Make sure your Universal Account Number is linked with your Aadhaar / PAN for seamless access to services like withdrawals, transfers and corrections.
  4. Employer Contribution: Verify that your employer has made the necessary contributions to your EPF account, as this affects the functioning of your UAN.
  5. Correct EPF Member ID: Ensure that your EPF member ID is correctly mapped to your UAN. If you’ve changed jobs, your previous and current EPF IDs should be linked to the same Universal Account Number.
  6. Access to Portal: Once activated, you can log in to the EPFO portal, where you can check your balance, request transfers and make withdrawals.

Solving Common Issues During UAN Activation Online

  • Ensure that your UAN is linked to your PF account number and Aadhaar.
  • Verify that your mobile number is registered with the EPFO.
  • Check if your employer has submitted your KYC details to the EPFO.
  • Contact your employer or the EPFO helpdesk for assistance if the issue persists.

Benefits of Having an Active UAN

  • Easy access to your PF account details and balance.
  • Simplified PF account transfers during job changes.
  • Option to file online claims for withdrawals and settlements.
  • Ability to update your personal information linked to your UAN.
  • Improved transparency and control over your PF account.

Circumstances for Money Withdrawal with an Active UAN

  1. Retirement: After reaching the age of 58 years, you can withdraw the entire sum from your PF account. An active UAN allows for quick access and processing of retirement benefits.
  2. Unemployment: If an employee has been jobless for two months, they can withdraw 75% of their PF balance using their active UAN account. If unemployment lasts longer than two months, the remaining 25% can be taken out as well.
  3. Partial Withdrawal: You can partially withdraw funds for specified reasons, such as:
    • Medical Treatment: Partial withdrawal is permitted in the event of a serious sickness for you or your immediate family.
    • Education: You may withdraw funds for your own or children’s higher education expenditures.
    • Marriage: Partial withdrawal for the marriage of oneself, siblings or children is allowed.
    • Home Loan Repayment or Property Purchase: Withdrawals are permissible for house loan repayment or property purchase.
  4. Resignation or Job Change: While it is typical to transfer the PF balance to a new company, you have the option to withdraw the whole PF balance after two months of leaving a job.
  5. Financial Emergencies: During a financial crisis, such as a pandemic or natural disaster, the government permits full withdrawals under such special conditions.
