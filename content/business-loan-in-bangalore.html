What is a Business Loan?

A business loan is a financial resource that businesses borrow from banks or other financial institutions to cover expenses they are unable to fund. Businesses can apply for a loan to meet various needs, including paying salaries and wages, promotional purposes, inventory, or launching new projects. Applying for a business loan, including an MSME loan, can help businesses secure the capital they need to cover their expenses, expand their operations, and grow their business. With a business loan,businesses can have the financial resources to seize new opportunities, stay competitive in the market, and achieve their goals.

Basic Eligibility Criteria

  1. The applicant should be between 21 and 65 years of age.
  2. The company should have a revenue generation of at least INR 10 lakhs and must have registered profits in the last financial year.
  3. The business should have an established track record. The minimum business vintage and the minimum business experience that we require are 1 year and 2 years respectively. Businesses with a stable record are more likely to have their loans approved.
  4. We consider the outstanding debts of a business before approving the business loan application and the amount sanctioned. We give utmost importance to the repayment ability of a business before sanctioning the loans.
  5. Although we sanction business loans to those having a CIBIL score of more than 650, a score of 700 or more is desirable. Better creditworthiness means better credit terms to the applicant from our end.

The applicant should not have defaulted on a previous loan obligation.
