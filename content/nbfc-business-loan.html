What Are NBFC Business Loans?

NBFC (Non-Banking Financial Company) business loans are unsecured loans provided by RBI-registered lenders outside the traditional banking ecosystem. Designed for fast-growing MSMEs, these loans come with:

  • Minimal documentation
  • Faster approvals
  • Flexible EMI plans

100% digital application process
They're ideal for meeting urgent needs like working capital, inventory purchase, or expansion, without the delays of traditional bank loans.

Top 4 Rejection Reasons Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) Q1: What is the difference between an NBFC and a bank loan? Ans: NBFCs offer faster, more flexible business loans with fewer documentation requirements compared to banks. They also cater to businesses with limited credit history, making them ideal for MSMEs. Q2: Can I get an NBFC loan without collateral? Ans: Yes, most NBFC business loans are unsecured. You can access funding based on your business turnover, banking history, and digital documentation—no need to pledge assets. Q3: What is the maximum loan amount I can get from an NBFC? Ans: Depending on your eligibility, you can get up to ₹50 lakhs. The final sanctioned amount depends on your turnover, business stability, and repayment history. Q4: Is the application process entirely online? Ans: Yes, with FlexiLoans and most modern NBFCs, you can apply, upload documents, and receive disbursal—all online. Q5: How soon can I expect funds after applying? Ans: Loan approval typically takes 24–48 hours, and disbursal can happen within 2–3 business days after verification. Q6: What if I have a low credit score? Ans: NBFCs often focus on cash flows rather than just credit scores. If your bank statement reflects steady income and transactions, you may still qualify. Q7: Are NBFC loans safe and RBI-regulated? Ans: Yes. All lending NBFCs on platforms like FlexiLoans are RBI-registered and follow stringent compliance and security guidelines.
