Documents Required for Instant Aadhaar Loan:

There are some documents required for an instant Aadhaar Card Loan for both salaried and self-employed individuals. If these documents are valid, the process of making a loan application becomes much easier.

Identity Proof (Anyone):

  • PAN Card (This is the most important ID proof)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Valid Voter ID
  • Valid Indian Passport
  • Valid Driver's License

Address Proof (Anyone):

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Valid Indian Passport
  • Valid Driver's License
  • Valid Voter ID
  • Electricity, gas, or water bill dated within the last 60 days.

Income Proof:

  • Bank statements for the last three months of your payment account in PDF format, indicating salary credits.

Please verify that the documents in the above list are valid when you submit your loan application. Additionally, the amount of documentation needed will be minimal if you are able to complete the KYC process during the application flow.
