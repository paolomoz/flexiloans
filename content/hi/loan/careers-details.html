About the Position:

As we scale, we are looking for a Product Analyst who will shape the direction of FlexiLoans Products by using data-driven insights to identify opportunities and prioritise product and engineering initiatives. This role will work heavily cross-functionally with various departments such as Product, Engineering, Business and Marketing in identifying key usage trends on existing features and new product launches to help drive innovation, feature adoption, and user growth

Key Responsibilities includes:

  • Develop data-driven business insights and narratives to identify opportunities, generate new hypotheses, and define product strategies through a deep understanding of the data, our customers, and our business
  • Design and analyze experiments to measure the impact of new product features and drive decision-making
  • Define key metrics mapping product performance to business success
  • Collaborate with engineers to ensure and improve data access and reliability
  • Work closely with founders to build product roadmap & philosophy

Candidate's Profile:

  • 2 years of B2C product management / product analyst or entrepreneurial experience
  • Proficient in SQL and other BI tools
  • High Ownership, Execution bias, High creativity quotient and user empathy
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills to analyze large quantum of datasets
  • Ability to work independently and bring innovative solutions to the team
  • B.E/ B.Tech with 2 years of experience in similar role
