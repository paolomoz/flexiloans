Save yourself from online fraud!

Here are some safety tips to remember

Never pay money

Never pay money to anyone claiming to process your application faster and reduce your interest rate. Remember Flexiloans doesn’t charge money to process your application.

Always verify the sender’s email

Never respond to emails requesting your personal information, open files and click on any links in such email before verifying the sender.

Never share OTP with anyone

Never share OTP with anyone. Remember Flexiloans representative will never ask for OTP over call or message.

Always verify the caller’s identity

Never share your bank, PAN and Aadhaar details with unknown callers claiming to be from Flexiloans. Always verify the caller’s identity before details.

If you have experienced any of the following, please report at
