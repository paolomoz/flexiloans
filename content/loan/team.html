Meet our Founders

Manish Lunia

Co-Founder

Manish Lunia is a seasoned entrepreneur with over two decades of experience across financial services, mergers & acquisitions, and corporate strategy. Before founding FlexiLoans, he held leadership roles at renowned organizations such as the Aditya Birla Group and Tata Teleservices for over a decade, and worked across "0-to-100" journeys in these organizations. A strong believer in the power of financial inclusion, Manish has served as Co-Chair for Fintech at FICCI and is currently the President of the fintech association Unified Fintech Forum (UFF), formerly DLAI, where he works closely with regulators, banks, and fintechs to shape responsible digital credit practices in India.
A Chartered Accountant and a Dean’s Lister MBA from the Indian School of Business (ISB), Manish is widely recognized for his insights in fintech and has spoken at key industry forums and global investor conferences.

Ritesh Jain

Co-Founder

Over two decades of experience in startups, finance and technology domains. CFO at housing.com. Implemented and integrated 12+ IT systems at Tata Teleservices-including Fraud management, SAS, Cognos, CRM, and Business intelligence tools. Banking and International stints with Citibank and Starwood Hotels. Built, scaled and managed teams of over 100 people. MBA from ISB, India (Dean’s List) and Chartered Accountant.

Deepak Jain

Co-Founder

Deepak Jain is a seasoned entrepreneur and finance professional with over two decades of experience across investment banking, consulting, and corporate strategy. Known for his deep expertise in financial services, he has led and structured transactions worth over USD 10 billion across multiple sectors.
A Chartered Accountant and an alumnus of the Indian School of Business, he combines strong financial acumen with a passion for technology-led innovation. His leadership has earned him recognition among India’s top young business leaders, including being featured in Fortune India’s "40 Under 40" and Dell x Observe Now’s "Top 40 Tech Entrepreneurs." Beyond his executive role, Deepak actively contributes to India’s startup ecosystem as Vice Chairman of the JITO Angel Network, and is a founding member of several entrepreneurial communities, including TEAMS and TiE Mumbai’s Fintech SIG.
