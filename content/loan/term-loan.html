What is a Term Loan?

A term loan is a type of business loan where you, as an applicant, borrow a certain amount of money and repay it in regular installments over a specific period. Businesses apply for term loans to get financing for expansion, buying assets, or working capital. These loans can be short-term loans (under 1 year), medium-term (1–3 years), or long-term (above 3 years).

Unlike revolving credit facilities, term loans provide a lump sum amount upfront that is paid back over time through scheduled payments. This structure offers predictability and helps businesses plan their finances effectively. The fixed nature of repayments makes MSME term loans particularly suitable for businesses with stable cash flows and specific growth objectives.

Example of Business Term Loan

Suppose you run a business that manufactures clothes and apparel. You need business financing of ₹10 lakh to buy machinery that helps you print designs. You apply for a term loan of ₹10 lakh for 2 years at an interest rate of 12%. You repay this business loan with a fixed monthly EMI over 2 years.

FAQs about Term Loan for Business

What is the interest rate for a term loan?

FlexiLoans term loan interest rates start from 12% per annum. The exact rate applicable to your business depends on factors including loan amount, tenure, business vintage, turnover, and credit profile.

Is there any processing fee?

Yes, a processing fee is applicable for term loans, and it ranges between 2-3% of the loan amount. The exact percentage will be communicated before loan confirmation.

How soon will the loan be disbursed?

Upon complete documentation and approval, term loans are typically disbursed within 48 hours. Some applications may be processed even faster based on the profile and documentation.

Can I prepay my term loan?

Yes, FlexiLoans offers prepayment options. You can make partial or full prepayments after completing a minimum number of EMIs as specified in your loan agreement. Prepayment charges, if any, will be mentioned in your loan terms.

Will applying for a term loan impact my credit score?

When you apply for a term loan for business, we conduct a credit check, which may cause a minor, temporary dip in your credit score. However, regular and timely repayment of your loan can positively impact your credit score over time.

What happens if I miss an EMI payment?

Missing an EMI payment results in late payment charges and can negatively impact your credit score. If you anticipate difficulty in making a payment, we recommend contacting our customer service team proactively to discuss potential solutions.

Can I get a term loan for a new business?

FlexiLoans typically require businesses to have at least 12 months of operational history. However, we do have special programs for relatively new businesses with strong potential. Contact our team to discuss your specific situation.

How is a term loan different from an overdraft facility?

The primary difference between term loans vs overdraft loans is in their loan amount and repayment structure. A term loan for business provides a lump sum amount upfront with fixed repayment schedules. An overdraft facility allows you to withdraw funds as needed up to a predetermined limit and pay interest only on the amount utilised.

Does FlexiLoans offer term loans for startups?

We have specialised startup financing options for businesses with at least 6 months of operational history and demonstrated revenue potential. Eligibility criteria for startup loans may differ from standard term loans.

Can I apply for multiple term loans simultaneously?

While it's technically possible to apply for multiple loans, we recommend discussing your complete funding requirements with our loan advisors. They can help structure an optimal financing solution that addresses all your business needs without over-leveraging your business.
