We Believe

Whatever your background, you’ll feel like you belong here. Your unique contribution will make a real impact on our customers and stakeholders.

FlexiLoans is committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workforce. We believe that no matter who you are or how you identify, you deserve respect, empathy and equal opportunity to succeed. We work hard to ensure everyone at FlexiLoans— and everyone we work with—is treated this way. Our diverse workforce comes from many different backgrounds, with different identities and beliefs.

Championing employment for individuals with disabilities

To find best-diverse talent, we actively partner with organizations that reach underrepresented communities like individuals with disabilities. Today they play a crucial role in our business success.

Pride at FlexiLoans - Supporting LGBTQ Community

Bring your whole self is the mantra we have at FlexiLoans. We strive to ensure an inclusive environment for all employees regardless of their sexual orientation, gender identity or expression.

Hear what our employee has to say on this

As a queer person, I and many like me always lookout for a workspace where your colleague or your peers are not bothered about your sexual orientation they accept you on a human level and appreciate your hard work it’s as simple as that. Working with FlexiLoans, I can vouch that it’s a safe space for the LGBTQI community as it’s been more than a year now and I never ever have seen anyone disrespecting anyone biased on their orientation and this is because one of our core value is “Respect”. At the end would like to quote Chief Justice of India Dipak Misra & Justice AM Khanwilkar “I am what I am. So take me as I am. No one can escape from their individuality."

Gender Diversity

We take pride in our journey on Gender Diversity. Today, we have more than 38% of employees being women in significant roles from Vice President levels to technical roles in the Product and Tech team. In 2019, we pledged during International Women’s day to infuse more women’s talent and provide them conducive work culture to succeed in their role.
