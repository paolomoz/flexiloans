Documents Required for a Business Loan

Individuals must maintain the following paperwork when applying for an unsecured business loan and satisfy the qualifying requirements. The loan application is subject to rejection if there is any inconsistency in the documentation.

  • Address proof (e.g., passport, Aadhaar card, lease agreement)
  • Know-Your-Customer (KYC) documentation
  • Income tax return (ITR) forms
  • Bank statements over the previous six months
  • A copy of the Partnership Deed or Declaration of a Sole Proprietorship
  • Copy of the last two years' income calculation, balance sheet, and profit and loss statement
  • Memorandum copies
  • Articles of incorporation
