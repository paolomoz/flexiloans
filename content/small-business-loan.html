How to get a small business loan approved online?

Small companies may obtain funds via debt or equity. Although equity constitutes mutual ownership and does not include repayment. Convincing the lender that your company can successfully repay the loan increases the chances of your loan request being approved.

  • Requirements

Lenders usually demand specific requirements for Business Loan. Do not be afraid to inquire about the terms and conditions. Lenders appreciate consistent cash flow, accurate accounting, and maintaining sufficient financial reserves.

  • Business plan

To qualify for a loan, you should have a solid business plan. It serves as an introduction to your business. A good business plan should be able to articulate the business's priorities, purpose, goals, and strategies for fulfilling those goals. Keep in mind that a solid strategy indicates that the venture is going to succeed. A good business plan increases the chances of loan approval.

  • Your intent

Lenders analyze the purpose of your loan. For instance, if you want to buy equipment, you must apply for an equipment loan. But if you need funds to keep the company going while waiting for payments from your debtors, it is essential to qualify for a short-term business loan. Thus, specifying the purpose of the loan enables the lenders to decide if the requested amount will fulfill your needs.

  • Healthy credit score

Nowadays, all lenders shall validate each applicant's eligibility for a loan. It is essential to review your credit records. A credit rating of 700 or higher is outstanding. If your credit score is less than 680, getting a loan can be difficult. In that case, you can consider a revenue-based loan as an option. If your credit score is significantly less, work diligently to improve the score to apply for any small business loan.

  • Financial statement

Obtaining a loan for your company becomes simpler if you have updated financial records. Banks will review your financial statements and cash flows to determine whether you are capable of repaying the loan or not. If you have not been maintaining these documents, start doing it now.

  • Requested loan amount

While applying for a loan, it is important to do extensive analysis to ensure that you do not apply for any extra loan amount. Undervaluing can give rise to financial difficulties in the future. If you do not make a proper analysis, the lender may think that your business idea is not good enough to generate profit. To eliminate both of these concerns, understand your business needs and make accurate financial forecasts.

Documents Required for Small Business Loan

  • Personal KYC: PAN card
  • Residential Address Proof (Anyone): Rent agreement, driver's license, Voter's ID, Ration card, Aadhaar Card, Passport
  • Banking: Last six months' current account bank statement
  • Business KYC (Anyone): GST registration certificate, shops and establishment certificate
  • Financial documents (For loans greater than 20 Lakhs): 2 years Audited financials, Last 2yrs ITR, GST Returns of 6 months
