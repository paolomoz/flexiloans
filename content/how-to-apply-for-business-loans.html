Understanding Business Loan Eligibility Criteria

  • Business should be operational for at least 1-3 years
  • Minimum annual turnover of ₹10 lakh to ₹50 lakh, depending on the lender
  • Credit score of 700 or above

Some lenders offer specialized loans like business loans for women entrepreneurs with relaxed eligibility norms.
