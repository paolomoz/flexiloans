Documents Required for Hassle-Free Business Loan Processing

While applying for a business loan, online ensure you should have the following essential documents:

1. Identity Proof of Applicant(s)

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID Card
  • Passport
  • Driving License

2. Address Proof of Applicant(s)

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID Card
  • Passport
  • Utility Bills (Electricity, Phone, Gas, etc.)
  • Ration Card
  • Rent Agreement or Registered Lease Deed

3. Business Proof and Vintage

  • Business Registration Certificate
  • Partnership Deed (for Partnership Firms)
  • Memorandum and Articles of Association (for Private Limited Companies)
  • Udyog Aadhaar / MSME Registration Certificate
  • Shop and Establishment Act Certificate
  • Factory Registration Certificate
  • SSI / MSME Registration Certificate
  • Import Export Code Certificate
  • GST Registration
  • Proof of Business Continuity (ITR, Trade License, Bank Statement, etc.)

4. Business Turnover and Cash Flow Documents

  • Latest 2 Years Audited Financial Statements (Balance Sheet, Profit and Loss Account, etc.)
  • Latest 2 Years Income Tax Returns of the Firm and Directors / Partners
  • Latest 6 Months Bank Account Statements of Firm
  • Processing of Order Book and Sales
  • Provisional Financial Statements for the Current Year

5. KYC Documents of Directors / Partners / Proprietor

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport Size Photographs

How to Submit Business Loan Documents?

You can submit documents for business loans digitally using the online method.

  • Step 1: Visit the Website or App: Visit our official website, FlexiLoans, your preferred online lender, or download mobile app from the Google Play Store.
  • Step 2: Fill Out Application: Navigate to the ‘Business Loan Eligibility’ page and fill out your application form with necessary details, such as name, mobile no, business information, and GST details etc.
  • Step 3: Upload Documents: Scan and upload key documents such as current account bank statements, KYC documents, and business KYC documents. Ensure each document is clear and legible.
  • Step 4: Credit Analysis: After assessing your business results, operations, and credit history, we will calculate the loan amount you are eligible for and the applicable interest rate. Our loan officers will be available to assist you in making the right decision.
  • Step 5: Loan Disbursement: We will share the loan agreement after document verification and loan approval. Once the agreement is signed, the fund will be disbursed to the applicant’s account within 48 hours.
