Documents Required for Business Loan in Delhi NCR

The documents required for a business loan in Delhi NCR may vary depending on the lender and the type of loan. However, some of the common documents that you may need to submit are:

  • Identity Proof: You must furnish a valid government-issued identity document like an Aadhar Card, Passport, or Driver's License.
  • Address Proof: Furnish your utility bill (electricity, water, or gas), rental agreement, or property tax receipt.
  • Business Existence Proof: You must submit a business registration proof, certificate of incorporation, partnership deed, or shop and establishment certificate
  • Legal Documents: Any legal documents, such as licenses, permits, or agreements relevant to your business.
  • Income Tax Returns: Your business's income tax returns for the previous financial years showcase your earnings, validating your repayment capacity.
  • Bank Statements: Include your previous six months' business bank statements for a specified period to display your financial transactions and cash flow.
  • Financial Statements: This comprises your Profit and Loss Statement, Cash Flow Statement, and Balance Sheet. These documents offer a snapshot of your financial status. If you are applying for a loan amount greater than ? 20 lakh, you must also provide your financial documents, such as audited financials, ITR, and GST returns.
  • GST Registration: If applicable, provide your GST registration certificate to demonstrate compliance with tax regulations.
  • Collateral Documents: In case you are applying for a secured business loan and pledging commercial property or assets, present ownership documents for verification.
  • Business Plan: A well-structured business plan outlining your company's objectives, operations, financial projections, and repayment strategy is crucial.
  • Project Report: A project report outlining the details and feasibility is essential for specific purposes like expansion or acquisition.
  • Debt Obligations: Details of existing loans or debts, if any.
